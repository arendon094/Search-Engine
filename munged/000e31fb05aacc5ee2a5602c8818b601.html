Auston Rotherham

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's Polo
Silver 1908 London Team competition

Auston Morgan Rotheram (June 18, 1876 – February 24, 1947) was a British/Irish polo player who competed in the 1908 Summer Olympics.

He was a member of the British polo team Ireland, which won the silver medal.
