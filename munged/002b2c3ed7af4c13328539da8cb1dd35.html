Richard Wainwright (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Richard Wainwright may refer to:

  • Richard Wainwright (1918–2003), British politician and businessman
  • Richard Wainwright (American Civil War naval officer) (1817–1862), Commander in the Union Navy during the American Civil War
  • Richard Wainwright (Spanish-American War naval officer) (1849–1926), officer in the United States Navy during the Spanish-American War
  • Richard Wainwright (World War I naval officer) (1881–1944), officer in the United States Navy during World War I, awarded the Medal of Honor
