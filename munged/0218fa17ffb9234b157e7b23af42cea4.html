April 4 (Eastern Orthodox liturgics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apr. 3 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Apr. 5

All fixed commemorations below celebrated on Apr. 17 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Saint George of Mount Malcon, monk, in the Peloponnesus
  • Saint Zosimas of Palestine, monk
  • Virgin-martyr Pherbutha of Persia, with her sister and her servants
  • Saint Theonas, Metropolitan of Thessalonica
  • Saint Zosimas of Vorbosomsk, abbot
  • Hieromartyr Nicetas the Albanian, of Mount Athos
  • Saint Joseph the Much-Ailing of the Kiev Caves

[edit] Other Commemorations

  • Icon of the Most Holy Theotokos "The Life-giving Spring"
  • Repose of Elder Savvas of Mount Athos (1908)
