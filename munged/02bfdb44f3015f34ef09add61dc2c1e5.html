South West Cape (Tasmania)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

South West Cape is a cape at the south-west corner of Tasmania, located at 43°34′16″S, 146°01′53″E.

It lies on the eastern corner of the Southwest National Park approximately 140 kilometres (87 mi) south of Hobart in Tasmania, and about 65 kilometres (40 mi) west and a little north of South East Cape.
