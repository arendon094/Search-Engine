Gmina Wiślica is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Busko County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Wiślica, which lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south of Busko-Zdrój and 60 km (37 mi) south of the regional capital Kielce.

The gmina covers an area of 100.58 square kilometres (38.8 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 5,690.

[edit] Neighbouring gminas

Gmina Wiślica is bordered by the gminas of Busko-Zdrój, Czarnocin, Nowy Korczyn, Opatowiec, Pińczów and Złota.
