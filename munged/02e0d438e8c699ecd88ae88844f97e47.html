Zemianske Podhradie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Nové Mesto nad Váhom District in the Trenčín Region

Zemianske Podhradie is a village and municipality in Nové Mesto nad Váhom District in the Trenčín Region of western Slovakia'.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1397.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 248 metres and covers an area of 8.231km². It has a population of about 783 people.
