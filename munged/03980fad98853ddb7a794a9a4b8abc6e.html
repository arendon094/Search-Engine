Gingal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The gingal was an enormous musket, usually fired from a wall, used by the Chinese and Indians in the 19th century.

Perrett, Bryan (2000). Gunboat!. Cassel & Co, pp. 39. ISBN 0-304-35670-0.
