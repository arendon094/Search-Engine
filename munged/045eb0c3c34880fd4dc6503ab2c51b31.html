SMPTE 356M Television specification for a professional video format, it is composed of MPEG-2 Video 4:2:2 I-frame only and 8 channel AES3 audio streams. These AES3 audio usually contain 24 bit PCM audio samples. It is possible to find video bitrates of 30, 40 and 50 MBit/s.

D10 is described in the document SMPTE 356M.

D10 is also called IMX by Sony.
