Hadrothemis is a genus of dragonfly in family Libellulidae. It contains the following species:

  • Hadrothemis camarensis
  • Hadrothemis coacta
  • Hadrothemis defecta
  • Hadrothemis infesta
  • Hadrothemis scabrifrons
  • Hadrothemis versuta
