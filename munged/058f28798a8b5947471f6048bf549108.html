Boards of Education in India

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The following boards of education have been recognized by Government of India

  1. Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education
  2. Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate Education
  3. Assam Board of Secondary Education
  4. Assam Higher Secondary Education Council
  5. Bihar School Examination Board
  6. Bihar Intermediate Education Council
  7. Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination
  8. Goa Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education
  9. Gujarat Secondary Education Board
  10. Haryana Board of Education
  11. Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education
  12. J&K State Board of School Education
  13. Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board
  14. Karnataka Board of the Pre-University Education
  15. Kerala Board of Public Examinations
  16. Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education
  17. Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education
  18. Manipur Board of Secondary Education
  19. Manipur Council of Higher Secondary Education
  20. Meghalaya Board of School Education
  21. Mizoram Board of School Education
  22. Nagaland Board of School Education
  23. Orissa Board of Secondary Education
  24. Orissa Council of Higher Secondary Education
  25. Punjab School Education Board
  26. Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education
  27. Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education
  28. Tamil Nadu Board of Higher Secondary Education
  29. Tripura Board of Secondary Education
  30. West Bengal Board of Secondary Education
  31. Central Board of Secondary Education
