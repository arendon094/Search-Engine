Paul Mehlitz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's field hockey
Silver 1936 Berlin Team competition

Paul Mehlitz (born December 24, 1906, date of death unknown) was a German field hockey player who competed in the 1936 Summer Olympics.

He was a member of the German field hockey team, which won the silver medal. He played two matches as forward.
