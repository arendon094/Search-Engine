Michael Mauldin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named Michael Mauldin, see Michael Mauldin (disambiguation).

Michael Mauldin is a music producer and former president of Columbia Records. He is the father of hip hop musician Jermaine Dupri.
