Enrico Lo Verso

Born January 18, 1964
Palermo

Enrico Lo Verso (born January 18, 1964) is an Italian actor.

He studied acting at Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia and INDA|Istituto Nazionale del Dramma Antico.
