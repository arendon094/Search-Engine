Olga Olegovna Fyodorova (Russian: Ольга Олеговна Федорова, born July 14, 1983) is a Russian sprinter who mainly competes in the 100 metres. Her greatest success has come in relay races, where she has Olympic silver medal to her name.

She was born in Sverdlovsk Oblast.

Olga is studying at the Ural State Technical University in Ekaterinburg. She is currently a fourth-year student specializing in information technology at the chair "Multimedia Technology" of the IMTEM department.
