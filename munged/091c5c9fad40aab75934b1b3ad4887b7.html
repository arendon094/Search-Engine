Galisancho

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Galisancho is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca, western Spain, part of the autonomous community of Castile-Leon. It is located 30 kilometres from the provincial capital city of Salamanca and has a population of 532 people.

[edit] Geography

The municipality covers an area of 22.85 km².

It lies 820 metres above sea level.

The post code is 37891.
