Kenneth Stuart

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brigadier Kenneth Stuart
Credit: National Film Board of Canada. Photothèque / Library and Archives Canada

Lieutenant-General Kenneth Stuart (September 9, 1891 – November 3, 1945) CB DSO MC was a Canadian soldier and Chief of the General Staff, the head of the Canadian Army from 24 December 1941 until 27 December 1943.

[edit] Lieutenant-General Stuart's Major Appointments

1934 - 1938 Instructor at Royal Military College of Canada
1938 - 1939 Director Military Operations & Director Military Intelligence
1939 - 1940 Commandant of Royal Military College of Canada
1940 - 1941 Deputy Chief General Staff
1941 Vice Chief of the General Staff
1941 - 1944 Chief of the General Staff
1944 Acting General Officer Commanding, 1st Canadian Army, England
1944 Chief of Staff Canadian Military Headquarters, England
