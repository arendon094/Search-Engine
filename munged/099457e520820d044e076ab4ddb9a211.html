Getaway (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also The Getaway, the name of films, novels, songs and video games

Getaway or Get away may refer to:

  • Getaway, Australia's longest-running and most popular holiday and travel television program
  • Getaway (The Saint), a mystery novel by Leslie Charteris first published in the United Kingdom in 1932
  • Getaway (Transformers), a fictional character in the Transformers toy line and fiction.
  • Getaway in Stockholm, a series of illegal street racing movies
  • Getaway (album), an album by British rock band Reef.
  • "Getaway," a song by The Music from their 2003 album The Music
  • Getaway (Georgie Fame song), a song by Georgie Fame.
  • Getaway (Richie Blackmore song), a song by Ritchie Blackmore released as 1965 single.
  • Get Away (Christina Milian song), a single and song by Christina Milian
  • Getaway (Earth, Wind & Fire song), a single and song by Earth, Wind & Fire
  • Get Away (King's X song), a song by King's X
  • Get away (or getaway) is a popular term for someone who is in a situation, sometimes accuses another person or persons and escapes the problem.
