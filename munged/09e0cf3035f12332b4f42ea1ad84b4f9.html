Texas Across The River is a 1966 western movie comedy with Dean Martin and Joey Bishop. The film was directed by Michael Gordon. The movie seemed to be a spoof of ' How the West was Won'. Best quote from movie: Martin (to Bishop): "Why weren't you born a Comanche?" Bishop: "Mother run too fast."

Alain Delon as Baldazar
Rosemary Forsyth as Phoebe Ann Naylor
Joey Bishop as Kronk

Richard Farnsworth as Comanche Medicine Man
