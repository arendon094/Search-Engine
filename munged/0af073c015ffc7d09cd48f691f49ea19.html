Holme on the Wolds is a village in the East Riding of Yorkshire, England. It is situated approximately 6 miles north east of the market town of Market Weighton and 5½ miles north west of the market town of Beverley. It lies to west of the B1248 road.

Holme on the Wolds, picture by Ian Lavender.

It forms part of the civil parish of Dalton Holme.

The village forms part of and is run by the Dalton Estate, which is owned by the Hotham family.
