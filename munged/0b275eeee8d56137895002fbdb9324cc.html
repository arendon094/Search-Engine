Forsythe Audio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Forsythe Audio Systems was an American manufacturer of professional loudspeaker systems designed by Kenton Forsythe, it was located in Watertown MA. Forsythe Audio operated from approximately 1975 to 1979, it grew out of Kenton's work at DB Sound and was best known for it's SR215 dual 15-in bass horn - that fit through most doors. Kenton moved to EAW [[1]] after the company wound down in 1978.
