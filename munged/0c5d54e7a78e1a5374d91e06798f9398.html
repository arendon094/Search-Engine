Ishaq Isa Abedeen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ishaq Isa Abedeen (born 1 January 1988 in Kenya) is a Bahraini runner.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2005 World Youth Championships Marrakech, Morocco 4th 3000 m
2007 Asian Championships Amman, Jordan 3rd 5000 m
4th 10,000 m[1]
