Munising Falls

Munising Falls is a waterfall located in Munising in the western-most portion of the Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore in Alger County, Michigan. The falls drops about 50 feet over a sandstone cliff. The amount of water falling is relatively small. The falls can be accessed by trail.
