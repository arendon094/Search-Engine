Krzysztof Hołowczyc

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Krzysztof Hołowczyc, 2007

Krzysztof Hołowczyc (b. June 4, 1962 in Olsztyn, Poland) is a Polish rally driver.

He won the Polish Rally Championship in 1995, 1996, 1999 and the European Rally Championship in 1997.

He is now a Member of the European Parliament for the Civic Platform since December 2007. He sits in the EPP-ED group.
