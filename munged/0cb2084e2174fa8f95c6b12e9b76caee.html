The Lijadu Sisters, Taiwo and Kehinde Lijadu, are identical twin sisters from Nigeria. They produced an album called Double Trouble, which was released in 1984. One of their songs, "Orere Eljigbo," can be found on a double CD entitled Nigeria 70. The Lijadu Sisters were active singers and performers in the 1970s, but are apparently no longer professionally active.
