Ned Vaughn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ned Vaughn (born November 20, 1964 in Huntsville, Alabama) is an American actor known for his television work. He attended Lee High School and Birmingham-Southern College.[1]

[edit] Notable filmography

  • China Beach, 1989 - Corporal Jeff Hyers
  • The Hunt for Red October, 1990 - Seaman Beaumont (the Sonar trainee) - USS Dallas
  • Chips, the War Dog, 1990 - Mitch Wilson [1]
  • Star Trek: The Next Generation episode "Tapestry," 1994 - Ensign Cortin Zweller.
  • Apollo 13, 1995
  • The Tuskegee Airmen, 1995
  • Murder One - from 1995 to 1996.
  • JAG 1996, 2001.
  • Courage Under Fire 1996
  • The Beach Boys: An American Family as Al Jardine
  • The Climb 2002 as Michael Harris
  • 24 - terrorist Mitch Anderson for several episodes in 2005
  • Ray Gun 2007
  • Cane 2007 Bronson
