[edit] Governors

Governors of the Australian colonies:

  • Governor of New South Wales- Lachlan Macquarie
  • Lieutenant-Governor of Tasmania - David Collins

[edit] Events

  • January 1 - Lachlan Macquarie sworn in as governor of New South Wales.
  • January 8 - The Derwent Star and Van Diemen's Land Intelligencer, Australia's second newspaper and the first in Van Diemenâ€™s Land, begins publication.

[edit] Exploration and Settlement

  • October 6 - A town plan of Sydney was published, on which the streets were given new and permanent names, including Market, George, Park and Barrack Streets.


[edit] Births

  • William Haines, the first Premier of Victoria.

[edit] Deaths

  • March 24 - Lieutenant-Governor of Tasmania David Collins died suddenly in Hobart.
