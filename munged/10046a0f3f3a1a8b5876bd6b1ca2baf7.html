Billy Ficca

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billy Ficca is a punk rock drummer who has played with the bands Television, The Waitresses and The Neon Boys. He frequently performed with guitarist/vocalist Tom Verlaine and bassist Richard Hell.

He has also been featured on albums by Coyote Shivers, Dave Rave Group, Glen or Glenda, The Novellas, Eugene Ripper, Shane Faubert, Brian Ritchie and Lach and the Secrets. He is currently playing with Gary Lucas, Dylan Nirvana and the Bad Flowers & Gods and Monsters.
