Nottingham West

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nottingham West is a middle class subdivision in West Houston, near the Memorial area. The area is served by the Spring Branch Independent School District. The neighborhood consists of mainly 1 story homes. The area is heavily wooded and has stable home values.[citation needed]

Students in the neighborhood attend Wilchester Elementary, Spring Forest Middle School and Stratford High School.
