Johnny Lewis (baseball player)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Johnny Lewis was a MLB baseball player from 1964-1967 who played for the St. Louis Cardinals and New York Mets. He was signed as a free agent by the Detroit Tigers in 1959. His full name is Johnny Joe Lewis and he bats left and throws right and weighs 189 pounds and is 6'1 in height. His first game was on April 14, 1964 versus the Los Angeles Dodgers and his final game was on June 11, 1967. He was born on August 10, 1939 in Greenville, Alabama.
