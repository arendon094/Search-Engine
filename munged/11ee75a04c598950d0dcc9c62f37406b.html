Andriy Yarmolenko (born October 23, 1989 in Kiev, Soviet Union) is a Ukrainian football forward for Dynamo Kyiv in the Ukrainian Premier League. He came to Dynamo in the winter of 2007 from Desna Chernihiv, and was promoted to the main squad in May 2008, at the end of the 2007-08 season.
