Morne Nagel (born March 23, 1978) is a South African sprinter. Together with Corne Du Plessis, Lee-Roy Newton and Matthew Quinn he won a silver medal in 4 x 100 metres relay at the 2001 World Championships in Athletics.

Following the ruling of December 13, 2005 which retroactively disqualified Tim Montgomery and henceforth the American team, the South African team were promoted to gold medallists.

[edit] Personal bests

  • 100 metres - 10.13 (2002, 2003)
  • 200 metres - 20.11 (2003)
