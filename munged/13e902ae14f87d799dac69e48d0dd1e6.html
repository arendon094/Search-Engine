Chocolate: Deep Dark Secrets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chocolate:Deep Dark Secrets is a Hindi-language film released in September, 2005. It stars Sunil Shetty, Arshad Warsi, Tanushree Dutta, Irrfan Khan, Anil Kapoor and Emraan Hashmi in the lead roles, as well as Emma Bunton in a guest role. Music of the film was composed by Pritam. Director was Vivek Agnihotri. The film appears to have been a failed copy of the hollywood movie, The Usual Suspects
