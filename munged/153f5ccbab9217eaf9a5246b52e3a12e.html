Eva Loseth

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Eva Loseth is an actress who made many guest starring appearances in several popular television shows of the 1990s.

She guest starred in Saved by the Bell, Quantum Leap, Doogie Howser, M.D., Silk Stalkings, Weird Science, Diagnosis Murder and in the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine episode "Life Support".

Loseth's only motion picture credit to date is 1998's Art House.
