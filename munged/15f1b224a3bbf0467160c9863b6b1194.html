Paul Byrne may refer to:

  • Paul Byrne (footballer born 1972), formerly of Celtic F.C. and Southend United F.C.
  • Paul Byrne (footballer born 1986) of University College Dublin A.F.C.
  • Paul Byrne (academic), the president of Grant MacEwan College
  • Paul Byrne (politician), who ran in the Washington State Senate elections, 2006
  • Paul Byrne (musician), a drummer and percussionist with In Tua Nua and Máire Brennan
  • Paul Byrne (athlete), an Australian gold medalist at the 1994 World Junior Championships in Athletics
