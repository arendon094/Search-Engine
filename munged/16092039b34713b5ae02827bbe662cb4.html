St. Margarets Bay is a Canadian administrative district in Nova Scotia's Halifax Regional Municipality.

It is a rural area located on the southwestern part of Halifax County comprising those communities in the county located on the eastern and northern shores of St. Margarets Bay through to the county line, terminating at the community of Hubbards.
