Rowan Ramsey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rowan Eric Ramsey is the Liberal Party of Australia representative for the electoral division of Grey, which covers the vast geographical majority (92 percent) of South Australia, succeeding prior Liberal MP Barry Wakelin as of the 2007 federal election.
