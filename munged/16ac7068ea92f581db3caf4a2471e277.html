Bill Schaake was the 29th head football coach for Washburn University in Topeka, Kansas and he held that position for two seasons, from 1967 until 1968. His overall coaching record at Washburn was 2 wins, 16 losses, and 0 ties. This ranks him 36th at Washburn in terms of total wins and 39th at Washburn in terms of winning percentage. [1]
