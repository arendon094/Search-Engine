The 2007–08 Georgetown Hoyas men's basketball team is an NCAA Division I college basketball team competing in the Big East Conference representing Georgetown University. The Hoyas look to continue their 2006-07 season success in which they finished first place in the conference, won the conference tournament, and advanced to the semifinals in the NCAA tournament.
