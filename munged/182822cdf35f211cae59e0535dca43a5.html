Bardo (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bardo or Bardos can refer to:

  • Bardo, Buddhist concept of a transitional realm
    • Bardo Thodol, Tibetan funerary text
  • Bardo (group), British pop group that represented the United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest in 1982
  • Bardo Pond, American psychedelic rock band formed in 1991

[edit] Places

  • Le Bardo, suburb of Tunis
    • Treaty of Bardo, 1881 treaty between France and Tunisia
    • Bardo Museum of Roman antiquities
  • Bardo, Poland, town in Lower Silesia
  • Bardos, Pyrénées-Atlantiques, commune in France
  • Bardoňovo, municipality in Slovakia
  • Mala Prespa and Golo Bardo, region in Macedonia and Albania
