Christos Kythraiotis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Christos Kythraiotis is a professional cyclist from Cyprus. He rides for the Omonoia cycling team, which is based in Cyprus.

In 2007, he became the National Champion for Cyprus in both the Road Race and the Time Trial.
