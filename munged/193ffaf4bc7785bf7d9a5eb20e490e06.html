Frederikshavn Stadion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Frederikshavn Stadion is a multi-use stadium in Frederikshavn, Denmark. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home stadium of Frederikshavn fI. The stadium holds 15,000 people.
