John Ottenheimer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

John Ottenheimer (born 1953 in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador) is a Canadian politician. He represented the riding of St. John's East in the Newfoundland and Labrador House of Assembly from 1996 to 2007. He is a member of the Progressive Conservatives.

He did not run for reelection in the 2007 provincial election.
