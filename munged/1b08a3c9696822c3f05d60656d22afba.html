"Me Haces Falta" (English: "I Need You") is the second single from Jennifer Lopez's first full Spanish album Como Ama una Mujer[1].

[edit] Music video

The video for the single was shot June 13, 2007 in Los Angeles and was directed by Sanji. Director of Photography Eric Wycoff [2]

In the music video, which premiered on Univision's Primer Impacto on July 18, 2007, [3] we see Jennifer as an FBI agent on a stakeout targeting the man she is in love with.

[edit] Trivia

  • Unlike Qué Hiciste, the song didn't get mass promotion or a worldwide release.
  • The song featured at the end of an episode in Jennifer's Spanish mini-series, "Como Ama una Mujer"
  • A short making of the video appeared on MTV UK, despite the song not having an official release there.
