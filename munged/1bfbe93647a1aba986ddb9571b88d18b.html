Sullivan Stapleton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sullivan Stapleton is an Australian actor, who has appeared in many television series but is best known from his roles in Satisfaction and The Secret Life of Us.

He is the brother of actress Jacinta Stapleton and the real-life partner of actress Carla Bonner.
