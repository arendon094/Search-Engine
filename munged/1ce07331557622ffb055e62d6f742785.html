Matthew Pateman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the actor, see Matthew Pateman (actor).

Matthew Pateman is a professor of literature and expressive arts at the University of Hull.

Pateman received his Ph.D. from the University of Leeds, and became a teacher at the Scarborough unit of the University of Hull. He primarily teaches popular culture, using the television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer as a guideline. He is now Senior Lecturer in Culture and Media Studies.
