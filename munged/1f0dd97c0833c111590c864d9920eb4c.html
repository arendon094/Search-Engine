Little Common Albion F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Little Common Albion F.C. are a football club based in Bexhill, England. They joined the Sussex County Football League Division Two in 1977. For the 2007-08 season, they are members of the Sussex County Football League Division Three.
