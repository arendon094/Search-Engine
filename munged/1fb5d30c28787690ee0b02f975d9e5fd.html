I Come With Love

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"I Come With Love" is a song written by Harry Connick Jr, first recorded for his Christmas album Harry for the Holidays.

"I Come with Love" is a Catholic layman's view of Christ's life in three stages, paralleling the Holy Trinity.

According to the composer, inspired by a scene from the horror movie Damien: Omen II, the scene where the boy realized that he was the Antichrist, and how he must have felt. The song is about what Christ felt when he found out that he was different.
