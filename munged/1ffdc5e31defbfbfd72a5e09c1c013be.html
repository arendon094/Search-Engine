Richard Y. Stevens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Richard Y. Stevens is a Republican member of the North Carolina General Assembly representing the state's seventeenth Senate district, including constituents in Wake county, USA. A management consultant from Cary, North Carolina, Stevens is currently (2007-2008 session) serving his third term in the state Senate.
