Ahmed bin Saeed Al Maktoum

Sheikh Ahmed bin Saeed Al Maktoum (born 1958) is the chairman of Emirates Airline.

He is also International Life Vice President of Chelsea Football Club (Chelsea F.C.).

He is the son of former Emir (Ruler) of Dubai Sheikh Saeed bin Maktoum Al Maktoum, the brother of former Ruler of Dubai Sheikh Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum and uncle of the current Ruler of Dubai Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum He is a graduate of the University of Denver.[citation needed]
