Koishite Kimeru! (恋してキメル！, Kimeru is in Love!?) is a single by the Jpop vocalist Kimeru and was used as the fourth and current closing theme for the Japanese dub of the 2003 Ninja Turtles series. It was written and composed by "ROLLY". It was released by Nippon Crown Records in maxi and CD/DVD combo formats on January 23, 2008 in Japan only and is coupled with the song "Love Touch".
