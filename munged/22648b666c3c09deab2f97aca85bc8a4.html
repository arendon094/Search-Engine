Luis Carlos Petcoff Naidenoff (b. 13 July 1967, El Colorado) is an Argentine Radical Civic Union politician. He sits in the Argentine Senate representing Formosa Province.

Petcoff Naidenoff studied in El Colorado and Corientes, and graduated as a lawyer at the Universidad Nacional del Nordeste (UNNE). He worked as a lawyer in Formosa. He served as President of the Association of Young Lawyers 1996-98.

In 1999, Petcoff Naidenoff was elected as a Formosa city councillor and headed the Radical block, serving until 2003. In 2005 he was elected to the Argentine Senate. In 2007 he was a candidate for Governor of Formosa, with Ana Elena Caligaris as his running mate. He lost to Gildo Insfr치n.[1]
