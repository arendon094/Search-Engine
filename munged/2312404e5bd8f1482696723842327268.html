Jamestown, Fife

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jamestown is a hamlet in Fife,United Kingdom. The A90 road and the Fife Circle Line are two modes of transport in Jamestown. The nearest ferry is from Rosyth,and goes to Zeebrugge. Edinburgh Airport is the nearest airport.
