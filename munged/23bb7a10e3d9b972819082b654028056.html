Bean Settlement, West Virginia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bean Settlement is an unincorporated community in Hardy County, West Virginia, USA. According to the Geographic Names Information System, the Bean Settlement community has also been known as Asbury, Asbury Church, Bean's, and Fabius.
