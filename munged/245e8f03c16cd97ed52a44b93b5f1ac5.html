Lionel Roux

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lionel Roux (born April 12, 1973 in Lyon) is a former tennis player from France, who turned professional in 1991. He was French National Junior champion in 1991, but didn't win a single title (singles and/or doubles) during his pro career. The right-hander reached his highest individual ranking on the ATP Tour on March 13, 1995, when he became the number 48 of the world.
