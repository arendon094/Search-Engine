The 8 metre class was a sailing event held as part of the Sailing at the 1912 Summer Olympics programme. It was the second appearance of the event. Races were held from July 20, 1912 to July 22, 1912 off the coast of Nynäshamn.

[edit] Results

The first race was held on 20 July, the second on 21 July. 7 points were given for 1st place in each race, with 3 for 2nd place and 1 for 3rd place.
