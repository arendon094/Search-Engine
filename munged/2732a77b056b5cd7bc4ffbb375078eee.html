Son Pari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Son Pari is an Indian television programme. It is a story about a little girl named Fruty who receives a magical gem that when rubbed, summons a fairy named Son Pari and her friend Altu.

The story starts when Fruty's friend Bunty catches a dove to give his friend to do an experiment. This friend of Bunty and Bunty himself are not good people for they wanted to capture Son Pari and show her to the world and get lots of money and get rich. So Fruty takes the dove and sets it free. What Fruity doesn't know is the bird is actually Son Pari. She often comes to Earth by the permission of her fairy godmother called Pari Ma.

Fruty's mother was long ago dead and Fruty always goes to the terries to see the stars. she thinks her mother is in one of them and on one such night Son Pari comes to her and offers her friendship for Fruty once helped her. Fruty quickly accepts her friendship to get rid of her loneliness. Then starts the story of their strange friendship. Son Pari and Altu start meeting Fruty regularly. They also help her get rid of Ruby, a girl who wants to marry Fruty's father, Rohit, for the sake of his money and Ruby's brother helps Ruby but Son Pari and Altu make sure they don't succeed. Then Son Pari realizes that Fruty is in great danger for it is written on the prophcies(stars) that Fruty will kill Kali Pari (Cruela) who is one of the villains of this story.
