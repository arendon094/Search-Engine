Viscount Mountmorres of Castlemorres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title of Baron Mountmorres was created on 4 May 1756 for Hervey Morres, who on 29 June 1763 was created Viscout Mountmorres. Both titles were in the Peerage of Ireland. The third viscount began to carry the surname de Montmorency, and so all the other viscounts did. On the death of the seventh viscount in 1951, both the barony and the viscountcy became extinct.

[edit] Barons Mountmorres (1756)

  • Hervey Morres, 1st Baron Mountmorres (c.1706-1766) (created Viscount in 1763)

[edit] Viscounts Mountmorres (1763)

  • Hervey Morres, 1st Viscount Mountmorres (c.1706-1766)
  • Hervey Raymond Morres, 2nd Viscount Morres (c.1743-1797)
  • Francis Hervey de Montmorency, 3rd Viscount Mountmorres (1756-1833)
  • Hervey de Montmorency, 4th Viscount Mountmorres (1790-1872)
  • William Browne de Montmorency, 5th Viscount Mountmorres (1838-1880)
  • William Geoffrey Bouchard de Montmorency, 6th Viscount Mountmorres (1872-1936)
  • Arthur Herve Alberic Bouchard de Montmorency, 7th Viscoutn Mountmorres (1879-1951)
