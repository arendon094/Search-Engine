Qasim Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Qasim Park is situated near Azam Cng in Lalamusa, Gujrat District, Punjab, Pakistan. It is located along the G.T road made by Sher Shah Suri. It is mainly hired for some marriage functions taken out in the town. It has a canteen and a big hall. There is also a greenish little belt in the park. It is very much a most visited park of lalamusa in terms of functions. It is built by the owners of "Mughal Furniture". Most of the park work is originally looked after by Mirza Shehbaz Ahmad and sometimes by his other brothers.
