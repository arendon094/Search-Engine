Pranab

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pranab is an Indian given name, common among Bengalis, Oriya and also ultilized by Nepalis. It may refer to:

  • Pranab Mukherjee, the foreign minister for India
  • Pranab Roy, a former Indian cricketer
