John Miller (Missouri)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

John Miller (November 25, 1781– March 18, 1846) was an American publisher and politician from St. Louis, Missouri. He was Governor of Missouri and represented Missouri in the U.S. House. He was born in 1781 in what was then Virginia in present day Martinsburg, West Virginia, and died in 1846 in Florissant, Missouri. He is buried at the Bellefontaine Cemetery in St Louis, Missouri.
