A. Karunakaran

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A. Karunakaran(born 1971) is a Telugu film director.

[edit] Background

Before coming into Telugu Film Industry, his parents forced him to join polytechnics. After finishing polytechnics in 1989, he went to Madras. He became an assistant director to a few successful directors like Shankar.

[edit] Career

Karunakaran's first directorial film was Tholi Prema was a sensational hit. That film enhanced Pawan Kalyan's image as an actor. Since then, he as given no hit. Yuvakudu, Vasu, Balu ABCDEFG, and Happy disappointed at box office.

[edit] Filmography

  • Happy (2006)
  • Balu ABCDEFG (2005)
  • Vasu (2002)
  • Yuvakudu (2000)
  • Tholi Prema (1998)
