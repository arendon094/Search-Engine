Faun & Games is the twenty-first book of the Xanth series by Piers Anthony.

[edit] Plot introduction

A young faun discovers his friend has gone missing and thus, the tree that faun is bound to will wither and die. The hero wishes to at least save his friend's tree but in doing so, he risks his tree also.
