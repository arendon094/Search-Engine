Ramble Dove

Ramble Dove is a Burlington, Vermont-based supergroup comprising key members of that scene, instigated by the notorious Brett R. Hughes which includes ex-Phish bassist Mike Gordon. The band, which was born out of Hughes' weekly Radio Bean honky tonk jam sessions (http://www.honkytonktuesday.com/), performs honky-tonk and vintage country music. A great number of Vermont luminaries join Hughes on stage for these weekly jam sessions, including Mike Gordon, Page O'Connell, Gordon Stone, Scott Murawski, Marie Claire, Mark Ransom, Tyler Bowles, Joe Clearly and Neil Cleary just to name a few. In the summer of 2006, the group performed at Tennessee's Bonnaroo festival.

For more information see: http://www.glidemagazine.com/articles234.html
