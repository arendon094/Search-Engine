Chaotic Code Masters

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In Chaotic, Codemasters are moderators of the Battledromes, the battle arenas in Chaotic. Each Battledrome is also named after their respective Codemaster, except for the Betadrome which has no Codemaster. Since there are seven "official" Battledromes, it is assumed that there are seven Codemasters. Some players point to obvious evidence that the first letter in the codemasters names will spell out the word Chaotic, which gives a clue as to the as yet unknown codemasters. They can appear in a regular human form, however they are seen with their outfit (which is donned using a special Chaotic card) during Codemaster matches. So far six have been revealed, but only two have actually made an appearance in person.

[edit] Codemaster Crellan

  • Voice Actor: Marc Diraison

Codemaster Crellan is the Codemaster of the Crellan drome. His appearance is that of a young adult male. His first appearance was in Battle Lessons, where he appeared in his normal human form and in his Codemaster suit. In Battle Lessons, Tom discovered Crellan (human form) hanging (and losing his grip) from a cliff in RockRiver Canyon after Tom thought he spotted Maxxor. At that time, however, Tom didn't realize that the player that he rescued was a Codemaster. Afterwards, he offered to give Tom a few tips on how to be a good player without his Maxxor card. Both later met again at the Betadrome, where Tom unsuccessfully tried to defeat Crellan's single creature, Ursis, as five of his creatures were defeated by Crellan as Ursis. Then before the last battle of the match, Tom came to the realization that Crellan was actually a Codemaster, just before Crellan transformed into his Codemaster outfit. The two then fought the last battle of the match, in which Tom, as Tangath Toborn, managed to nearly defeat Ursis. However, Crellan managed to use Song of Translocation Mugic to change the location, and finished Tangath Toborn. Afterwards, Crellan disappeared when Tom rematerialized in the drome.

Tom later re-battles Codemaster Crellan after winning 7 battles in the Crellan Drome and manages to win thanks to the Mugic Klay and Krystella tricked him into scanning, much to their discontent. Tom thought he was scanning a Mugic called Modulation which was supposed to double his energy but it actually switches the disciplines (elemental capabilities) of the player's creature with that of the opponent's creature. During the match Tom ends up fighting against one of Crellan's strongest creatures, Cromaxx, a savage version of Maxxor. He wins by defeating Crellan's creature Ariak (whick had the ability to bring back other creatures) and then used Maxxor to battle Cromaxx at Kiru City. Tom's strategy was then to run into Maxxor's castle in order to gain a Mugician, which would allow him to cast a Mugic. He casts his new Modulation Mugic and it allows him to copy Cromax's abilities. With Crellan defeated, he rewards Tom with a Prexxor Chasm scan.

Smildon later makes a reference to him when Tom visited Prexxor Chasm.

[edit] Trivia

Many of Codemaster Crellan's creatures appear to have links to prehistoric times. Cromaxx has the appearance of a Neanderthal, while Ursis is a hairy, bear-like humanoid with vicious, jagged teeth, much like Ursus, the latin word for bear . Proboskar looks like a woolly mammoth and Smildon is probably a play on Smilodon.

[edit] Codemaster Amzen

  • Voice Actor: Emily Blau

Kaz mentions to Tom that he fought Codemaster Amzen once and lost as Chaor when Amzen used a BattleGear that turned him into a shock absorber.

[edit] Codemaster Tirasis

The Codemaster of the Tirasis drome. Battledrome Tirasis was said in passing when Kaz was saving Drew from all the players that wanted challenge him in the episode "Allmegeddon."

[edit] Codemaster Imthor

  • Voice Actor: Dan Green

Peyton mentions to Tom that he fought Codemaster Imthor once and lost when a tentacled creature Imthor played as constricted him.

Codemaster Imthor makes a brief battle appearance against Klay and beats Klay with a giant invisible creature in the episode in which Tom re-battles Codemaster Crellan. Considering the size of his invisible creature's footsteps, it was most likely a large unknown creature, but he may have been using Fortissimo Mugic.

In "Fighting Friendly", Tom and Kaz later faced off in the Imthor Drome to determine who will face off against Codemaster Imthor. After a battle where Tom as Najarin faced off against Kaz as Lord Van Bloot, Kaz accuses Tom of cheating after that claiming that he had a BattleGear underneath Najarin's cape, which would've been useless with Najarin unless he had a certain Mugic or knew Kaz's strategy. Imthor was sent down to look into this. Not sure about what to do after finding out that Tom didn't have the Mugic that was needed, Imthor states that he will discuss this matter with the other Codemasters, banning Tom from Chaotic Battles until further notice.

[edit] Codemaster Chirrul

  • Voice Actor: Annice Moriarty

Sarah mentioned to Tom that she fought Codemaster Chirrul once and lost as Junda when Chirrul trapped her in a bubble and spun her at high speeds. Sarah claims that she's still dizzy from that battle.
