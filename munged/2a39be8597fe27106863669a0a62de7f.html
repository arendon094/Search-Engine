Fantastic Four (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fantastic Four is the name of:

  • Fantastic Four, a superhero comic book from Marvel Comics, and the team appearing in it
  • Fantastic Four (film), a 2005 film based on the Marvel Comics series directed by Tim Story
    • Fantastic 4: The Album, the soundtrack of the 2005 film.
    • Fantastic Four (video game) from Activision, based on the 2005 film
  • Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer, a 2007 sequel to the 2005 film, introducing the Silver Surfer and Galactus.
    • Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer (video game), the video game based on the 2007 film
  • The Fantastic Four (film) is an unreleased 1994 film by Roger Corman, also based on the comics.
  • Fantastic Four (1967 TV series) is an old animated series based on the comics.
  • Fantastic Four (1978 TV series) is another animated series based on the comics, notable for not including The Human Torch.
  • Fantastic Four (1994 TV series) is an animated series as part of The Marvel Action Hour, also based on the comics.
  • Fantastic Four: World's Greatest Heroes is the current animated series based on the comics.
  • Fantastic Four (PlayStation game), a side-scrolling video game released in 1997.
  • Ultimate Fantastic Four, the Ultimate Marvel comic book variation on the classic team
  • Fantastic Four Adventures The Panini Comics comic that reprints for the UK Fantastic Four comics
  • The Fantastic Four : An R&B vocal group that recorded for Motown and several other labels during the 60's & 70's
  • Fantastic Four, a mixtape by Cassidy, Fabolous, Lil Wayne and Juelz Santana.

Fantastic Four may also mean:

  • Die Fantastischen Vier, a German hip hop group
  • The wide receiver combo of the Seattle Seahawks, consisting of Darrell Jackson, Bobby Engram, Nate Burleson, and Deion Branch.
  • The forward combo of FC Barcelona, consisting of Lionel Messi, Ronaldinho, Samuel Eto'o, and Thierry Henry.
