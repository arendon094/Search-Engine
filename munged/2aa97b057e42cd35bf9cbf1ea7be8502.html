Mak Pui Hin

Medal record
Women's Squash
Asian Games
Bronze 2006 Doha Individual

Mak Pui Hin (born 6 March 1978) is a squash player from Hong Kong, China who won a bronze medal at the 2006 Asian Games in the women's kumite -60 kg class.
