Spencer High School (Wisconsin)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Spencer High School is a public high school located in Spencer in Marathon County, Wisconsin.

Spencer High School has a multi-talented staff and has multiple extracurricular activities such as: football, basketball, softball, baseball, track & field, cross country, volleyball, wrestling, drama club, history club, facebook, forensics, FFA, FBLA, and more. For its size, it offers great opportunities for advanced education. The average size of graduating classes is about 40-60 students.
