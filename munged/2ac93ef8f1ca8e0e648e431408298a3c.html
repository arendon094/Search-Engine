Roughley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Roughley is a part of Sutton Coldfield, which is north of Birmingham in the West Midlands. It is north of Sutton Coldfield close to Mere Green and Four Oaks.

[edit] Transport

The 905 and 105 bus routes go to Roughley. The 604 stops at Little Sutton Road, which is within walking distance of Roughley.
