The arrondissement of Draguignan is an arrondissement of France, located in the Var department, in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. It has 12 cantons and 58 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Draguignan are:

  1. Callas
  2. Comps-sur-Artuby
  3. Draguignan
  4. Fayence
  5. Fréjus
  6. Grimaud
  7. Lorgues
  8. Le Luc
  9. Le Muy
  10. Saint-Raphaël
  11. Saint-Tropez
  12. Salernes
