Valea Seacă River (Olt)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are several tributaries of the Olt river having the name Valea Seacă River

  • Valea Seacă - tributary of the Olt River in the Rotbav area
  • Valea Seacă - tributary of the Olt River in the Sfântu Gheorghe area
