Yorusora ni YOUKISS!

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yorusora ni YOUKISS! (夜空に YOUKISS!) is the third solo photobook of japanese singer and actress Yuki Uchida, released in Japan in 1996, centering on her activity as a radio show hostess on her own weekly 30mn late show, "Yorusora (nightsky) ni YOUKISS!", from april 1994 to March 2001, on "Nippon Broadcasting". The book also contains text and poems.
