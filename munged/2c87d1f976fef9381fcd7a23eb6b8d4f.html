Levien Gymnasium

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Levien Gymnasium is a 3,408-seat arena at Columbia University in New York City. It is home to the Columbia Lions basketball team, and is also used for gym classes in between games. It is part of the Marcellus Hartley Dodge Physical Fitness Center.
