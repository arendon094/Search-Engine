Roman Catholicism in Dominica

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Roman Catholic Church in Dominica is part of the worldwide Roman Catholic Church, under the spiritual leadership of the Pope and curia in Rome. About 77% of Dominica's population of 70,000 are Catholic. The entire country is under the jurisdication of a single diocese, the Diocese of Roseau.
