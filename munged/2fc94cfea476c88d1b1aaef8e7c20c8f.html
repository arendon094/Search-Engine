Hirohiko Kakegawa (掛川裕彦, Kakekawa Hirohiko?) (born October 11, 1964) is an Japanese actor. He has starred in a number of anime seires, such as Transformers and Turn A Gundam[1]. He enjoys softball, ice-skating and tennis[2].
