Torre de' Roveri is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Bergamo in the Italian region of Lombardy, located about 50 km northeast of Milan and about 9 km east of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,134 and an area of 2.7 km².[1]

Torre de' Roveri borders the following municipalities: Albano Sant'Alessandro, Pedrengo, San Paolo d'Argon, Scanzorosciate.
