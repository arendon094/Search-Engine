The Monroes (Norwegian band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 This article does not cite any references or sources. (April 2008)
Please help improve this article by adding citations to reliable sources. Unverifiable material may be challenged and removed.

The Monroes was a Norwegian rock duo that held significant popularity in Norway in the 1980s. Their biggest hit was Sunday People.
