Cornell University Roller Hockey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cornell University Roller Hockey (CURH) is a collegiate inline hockey team based in Ithaca, New York. Big Red represents Cornell University and is a recognized club sport. Currently Big Red is governed by the National Collegiate Roller Hockey Association. Big Red competes in the Eastern Collegiate Roller Hockey Association (ECRHA) at the Division II level.
