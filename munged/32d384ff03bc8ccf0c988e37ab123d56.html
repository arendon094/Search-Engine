List of Chinese language schools in Taiwan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Taiwan has long been a destination for foreign learners of Chinese and is home to many Chinese language schools. Several schools also offer courses in Taiwanese, or less commonly Hakka and Cantonese.

Taipei

  • International Chinese Language Program (ICLP), an institute of National Taiwan University
  • Center for Chinese Language and Cultural Studies (CCLC), formerly the Mandarin Training Center, an institute of National Taiwan Normal University
  • Taipei Language Institute (TLI), an independent, privately-run school

Hsinchu

  • National Chiao Tung University Chinese Language Center

Tainan

  • National Cheng Kung University Chinese Language Center
