John Joe Rice

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

John Joe Rice (died 1 July 1970) was an Irish Sinn Féin politician. He was first elected as a Sinn Féin Teachta Dála (TD) at the 1957 general election for the Kerry South constituency. He did not take his seat in the Dáil as Sinn Féin ran on abstentionist ticket. He was one of four Sinn Féin TDs elected in the 1957 general election, the others were Ruairí Ó Brádaigh, John Joe McGirl and Eineachan Ó hAnnluain. He was defeated in the 1961 general election.
