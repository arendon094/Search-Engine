Judge Louis Gates House

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Judge Louis R. Gates House is located at 4146 Cambridge Street in Kansas City, Kansas. Clarence E. Shepard was the architect of the building. It was placed on the Kansas City, Kansas Historic Landmark on August 26, 1982. It was placed on the Register of Historic Kansas Places on July 3, 1979. It was placed on the National Register of Historic Places on December 1, 1980.
