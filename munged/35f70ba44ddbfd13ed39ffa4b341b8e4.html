Ron Shudra (born November 28, 1967(1967-11-28) in Winnipeg, Manitoba) is a professional ice hockey player who holds dual Canadian and British nationality. He was drafted 63rd overall by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1986 NHL Entry Draft and played ten games for them in 1987–88 season.

He currently plays for the Sheffield Scimitars in the English Premier Ice Hockey League.
