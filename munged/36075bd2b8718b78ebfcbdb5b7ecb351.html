[edit] Biography

Xavier Thomas was born in Paris, France to French parents. His father, Raymond Thomas is a famous French sport psychologist.

Thomas began his career as a journalist in IDG. After a one year training in the International Image and Sound Institute, he begins to work for cable televisions. Due to the difficulties to make a living, he began a new career as a photographer of writers. His works have drawn attention to art galleries. He has worked for Elle magazine, Paris Match and Le Monde.

In February 2007, he and his wife Clotilde decided to create a publishing house: Thomas Jeunesse.[1]
