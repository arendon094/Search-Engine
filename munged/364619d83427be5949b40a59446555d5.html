Bull (zoology)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bull or bull in zoology may refer to::

  • Uncastrated male cattle. This is the origin of most of the other meanings
  • A male of various other animal species, including buffalo, elephant, and whale. In English, the word "bull" alone is usually spoken to refer specifically to male cattle, with terms such as "bull elephant" disambiguating the term for other species. See also cow.
