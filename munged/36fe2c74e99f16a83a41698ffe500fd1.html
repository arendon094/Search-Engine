Bodzianske Lúky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Catholic church

Bodzianske Lúky (Hungarian: Bogyarét) is a village and municipality in the Komarno District in the Nitra Region of south-west Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1387.

[edit] Geography

The village lies at an altitude of 109 metres and covers an area of 5.166 km². It has a population of about 220 people.

[edit] Ethnicity

The village is about 95% Magyar, 5% Slovak.

[edit] Facilities

The village has a public library, and a football pitch.
