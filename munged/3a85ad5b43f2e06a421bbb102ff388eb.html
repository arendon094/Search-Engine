Lucky Bank of Taiwan Incorporated (第七商業銀行) is a bank based in Taichung, Taiwan. Founded in 1947 as the credit department of the Central District Co-operative in Taichung, it was renamed the Seventh Credit Co-operative of Taichung City in 1971. It adopted its present name in 1 September 1997.

The bank was acquired by the Cathay United Bank (國泰世華銀行) on 1 January 2007.
