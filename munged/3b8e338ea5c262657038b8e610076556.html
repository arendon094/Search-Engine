Kolonowskie [kɔlɔˈnɔfskjɛ] (German: Colonnowska, sometimes Kolonnowska, 1936–1945 Grafenweiler) is a town in Strzelce County, Opole Voivodeship, Poland, with 3,433 inhabitants (2004).
