1958 French Championships - Men's Singles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mervyn Rose defeated Luis Ayala 6-3 6-4 6-4 in the final to win the Men's Singles title at the 1958 French Championships.

[edit] Seeds

The seeded players are listed below. Mervyn Rose is the champion; others show the round in which they were eliminated.

  1. Ashley Cooper (Semifinalist)
  2. Neale Fraser (Quarterfinalist)
  3. Mervyn Rose (Champion)
  4. Budge Patty (Fourth round)
  5. Luis Ayala (Finalist)
  6. Jaroslav Drobny (Fourth round)
  7. Jacques Brichant (Semifinalist)
  8. Giuseppe Merlo (Quarterfinalist)
  1. Robert Keith Wilson (Second round)
  2. Orlando Sirola (Fourth round)
  3. Pierre Darmon (Quarterfinalist)
  4. Nicola Pietrangeli (Fourth round)
  5. Robert Haillet (Quarterfinalist)
  6. Paul Remy (Fourth round)
  7. Kurt Nielsen (Second round)
  8. Andres Gimeno (Fourth round)
