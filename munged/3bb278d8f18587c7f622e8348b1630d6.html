The Corvallis-Benton County Public Library is a public library located in Corvallis, Oregon, U.S. The library's vision statement is Bringing people and information together. It is part of a city/county system with branches in Alsea, Monroe, and Philomath as well as a bookmobile. [1] It has been listed as one of the top ten libraries in the country (1997, 1999, 2000) based on population served by Hennen's American Public Library Ratings.[2]
