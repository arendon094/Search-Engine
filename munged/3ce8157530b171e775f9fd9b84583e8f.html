Motor Lublin, (pron. IPA: [ˈmɔtɔr ˈlublin], are a Polish professional football team based in Lublin. The club were founded in December 1950 with their nickname The Yellow, White and Blues reflecting their official colours. They currently play in the Polish Second Division.

(till 1950) Metalowiec
(since 1950) ZKS Stal Lublin
(since 1957) Robotniczy Klub Sportowy Motor Lublin
(since 1998) Lubelski Klub Piłkarski
(since 2001) LKP Motor Lublin

[edit] Honours

  • First league
    • 9 seasons in the First league:
    • 1980/1981
    • 1981/1982
    • 1983/1984
    • 1984/1985
    • 1985/1986
    • 1986/1987
    • 1989/1990
    • 1990/1991
    • 1991/1992
  • Second league
    • 19 seasons in the Second league:
    • 1965/1966
    • 1968/1969 - 1971/1972
    • 1973/1974 - 1979/1980
    • 1982/1983
    • 1987/1988 - 1988/1989
    • 1992/1993 - 1995/1996
    • 2007/2008
  • Polish Cup
    • Quarterfinals in 1978/1979 and 1981/1982
  • Polish Championship U-19
    • Winners in 1971
  • UEFA Intertoto Cup
    • Participants in 1982 - played against Lyngby BK, MSV Duisburg and FC Luzern
