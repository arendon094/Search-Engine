Harry Rosenswärd

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's Sailing
Gold 1912 Stockholm 10 metre class

Harry Rosenswärd (born April 20, 1882 - died July 16, 1955) was a Swedish sailor who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.

He was a crew member of the Swedish boat Kitty, which won the gold medal in the 10 metre class.
