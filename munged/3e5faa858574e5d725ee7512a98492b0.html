Requiem is the title of several pieces of music typically associated with the Requiem Mass, including:

  • Requiem (Mozart), by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
  • Messa da Requiem, by Giuseppe Verdi
  • Requiem (Fauré), by Gabriel Fauré
  • Requiem (Berlioz), Grande Messe des Morts, by Hector Berlioz
  • Requiem (Rutter), by John Rutter

Requiem can also refer to:
