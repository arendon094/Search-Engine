Hraničné

Location of Stará Ľubovňa District in the Prešov Region.

Hrani is a village and municipality in Stará Ľubovňa District in the Prešov Region of northern Slovakia.

the wooden church of Hraničné

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1342.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 530 metres and covers an area of 7.543 km². It has a population of about 219 people.
