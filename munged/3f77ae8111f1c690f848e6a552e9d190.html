Josep Antoni Duran i Lleida

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Josep Antoni Duran i Lleida (born March 27, 1952 in Alcampell, Huesca) is a Spanish politician. Licenced in law, Duran began his politic career when he was chosen MP in the General Elections of Spain in 1979, becoming teniente de alcalde in the municipality of Lleida.

Currently Duran is an MP for Barcelona, chosen in the General Elections in 2004, and is a member of Convergència i Unió. Duran i Lleida is also the president of Unió Democràtica de Catalunya, a political party which is part of CiU, since 1987 and also from 1982 to 1984.
