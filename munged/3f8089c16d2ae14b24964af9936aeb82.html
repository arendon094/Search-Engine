Dinh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dinh may refer to:

  • A Vietnamese spirit house.
  • A Vietnamese family name (Vietnamese: Đinh, Simplified Chinese: 丁, Pinyin: Dīng). It is the Vietnamese version of the Chinese surname Ding.
  • Dinh Bo Linh (Đinh Bō Lïnh), considered the first king in the history of Vietnam.
  • The leader of a ka-tet in Stephen King’s Dark Tower novels.
