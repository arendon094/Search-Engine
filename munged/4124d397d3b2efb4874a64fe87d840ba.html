"Pheromone, My Lovely" is the eleventh episode of the television program Lois & Clark: The New Adventures of Superman.

[edit] Summary

An ex-lover of Luthor's sprays the Daily Planet with a pheromone-based perfume that makes everybody fall in love except Clark.

[edit] Writing and acting

[edit] Main cast

  • Dean Cain as Clark Kent/Superman
  • Teri Hatcher as Lois Lane
  • Lane Smith as Perry White
  • Tracy Scoggins as Cat Grant
  • Michael Landes as Jimmy Olsen
  • K Callan as Martha Kent
  • Eddie Jones as Jonathan Kent
  • John Shea as Lex Luthor

[edit] Recurring cast

  • Tony Jay as Nigel

[edit] Guest starring

  • Morgan Fairchild as Miranda

[edit] Production details

  • Lex recites a part of John Keats's Ode to Melancholy to Lois.
  • The title is a reference to the Raymond Chandler novel Farewell, My Lovely.
