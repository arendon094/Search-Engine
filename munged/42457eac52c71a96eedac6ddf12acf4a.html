Frederick Phillips

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's field hockey
Bronze 1908 London Team

Frederick Gordon Phillips (born March 13, 1884, date of death unknown) was a British field hockey player who competed in the 1908 Summer Olympics.

In 1908 he won the bronze medal as member of the team Wales.
