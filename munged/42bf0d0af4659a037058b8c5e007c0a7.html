Ashtabula Harbor Light
U.S. National Register of Historic Places
Location: Ashtabula, Ohio
Added to NRHP: 1983-08-04[1]
NRHP Reference#: 83001943[1]
MPS: U.S. Coast Guard Lighthouses and Light Stations on the Great Lakes TR
Governing body: Federal[1]

Ashtabula Harbor Light is a registered historic structure in Ashtabula, Ohio, listed in the National Register on 1983-08-04.

[edit] Historic uses

  • Military Facility
  • Water-Related
