Lomphat Wildlife Sanctuary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lomphat Wildlife Sanctuary is a heavily forested area straddling Ratanakiri, Mondulkiri, and Kratie provinces in Cambodia set aside by the Cambodian government as a wildlife preserve. With an area of 250,000 hectares, it is home to a number of wild animals such as buffalo, tiger, wild pig and bear.
