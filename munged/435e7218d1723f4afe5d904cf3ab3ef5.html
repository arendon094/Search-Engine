Clint Albright

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Clint Albright (born February 28, 1926 in Winnipeg, Manitoba) is a former centreman in the NHL for the New York Rangers. He played 59 games for the Rangers in the 1948–49 season.

[edit] Awards & Achievements

  • Memorial Cup Championships (1946)
