Baluchi (horse)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baluchi horse is a type of horse native to the Baluchistan, Sind and Punjab Provinces inPakistan.[1]

The Baluchi horse is usually bay, chestnut, or gray. They are light in build and generally have a fine head, long neck, strong but fine legs and ears that curve in so the tips of the ears touch. The Baluchi bears a strong resemblace to Indian Kathiawari breed. They are also thought to be related to the Barb through the Mali breed known as the Beledougou or Banamba.[1]

They are used for riding and light draft work.[1]
