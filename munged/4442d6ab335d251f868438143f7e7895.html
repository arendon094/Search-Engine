Kaoru Okubo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kaoru Okubo (大久保 薫, Ōkubo Kaoru?, born December 24, 1972) is a male Japanese music arranger for music featured in anime.

[edit] Music arranged

  • Asatte no Houkou
  • Bincho-tan
  • Cosmic Baton Girl Comet-san
  • D.C.S.S. ~Da Capo Second Season~
  • Daphne in the Brilliant Blue
  • Hand Maid May
  • IGPX
  • Kashimashi: Girl Meets Girl
  • Mai-Otome
  • My-HiME
  • Negima!
  • Negima!?
  • School Days
  • Strawberry Panic!
  • Tactical Roar
  • Zettai Seigi Love Pheromone
