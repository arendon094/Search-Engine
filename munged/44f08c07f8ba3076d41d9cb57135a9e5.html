SIGCHI Bulletin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The SIGCHI Bulletin is one of the two membership publications of ACM SIGCHI, the Special Interest Group on Computer Human Interaction. The other publication is interactions.

The Bulletin appeared quarterly until 1999 when it became bi-monthly, but returned to quarterly in 2005. Its first issue appeared in July 1982, though bearing the volume number 14, since it was a result of the renaming of the SIGSOC Bulletin, after SIGSOC (Special Interest Group on Social and Behavioral Computing) renamed itself to SIGCHI.

The Bulletin was a paper publication until October 1995, after which it was published simultaneously on paper and on the Web, until July 2003, when it became online-only.

In 2000, SIGCHI made interactions the member publication. Up until then members had had to subscribe to it separately. From that point, the Bulletin was published as a supplement to interactions.
