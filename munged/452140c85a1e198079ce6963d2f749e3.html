"Pimpin' All Over the World" is a 2005 single by rapper Ludacris featuring singer Bobby Valentino. It was taken from the album The Red Light District and was produced by Polow Da Don and Donnie Scantz. The song eventually became the most successful single from The Red Light District. The song features Ludacris rapping the verses throughout the song, and then accompanying Valentino on the chorus. The video of the song features them on islands drinking, watching women, and dancing with women. "Pimpin' All Over the World" was well received on the Billboard charts, reaching #9, but achieved its highest spot on Hot Rap Tracks, reaching #2. Its casually-misogynistic lyrics angered many women's rights groups. This song also includes a cameo from comic Katt Williams.
