Khorgo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Khorgo (Mongolian: Хорго) is an extinct volcano in the Arkhangai Province of Mongolia, on the northern slopes of the Khangai Mountains. It is part of the Khorgo-Terkhiin Tsagaan Nuur National Park, together with the lake Terkhiin Tsagaan Nuur.
