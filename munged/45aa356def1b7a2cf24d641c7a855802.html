Preferente Territorial de Catalunya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Preferente Territorial is the sixth level of competition of Spanish football in Catalonia. It is mainly organized by the Catalan Football Federation.

[edit] Competition System

It consists of 2 groups of 18 clubs each one.

3 teams are promoted to Primera Catalana and 6 are relegated to Primera Territorial de Cataluña.
