Amica Mutual Insurance is a mutual insurance company founded in 1907 and located in Lincoln, Rhode Island offering primarily auto, home, and life insurance. The company is licensed in 49 US states with most business concentrated in the Northeastern United States, Florida, Texas, and California. In 2006, the company reported over two billion in policyholder's surplus.
