The Newmarket GO Station is a train and bus station in the GO Transit network located in the Tannery Centre on Davis Drive East in Newmarket, Ontario in Canada. It is a stop on the Barrie line train service, and is 3 km from the Newmarket Bus Terminal on Davis Drive West.

There is a distinct difference between the Newmarket GO Station and the Newmarket GO Bus Terminal. The Newmarket GO Station is in the Tannery Center on Davis Drive and is specifically the stop for the GO Train service, while the Newmarket GO Bus Terminal is off of Eagle St. and is specifically the stop for the GO Bus. The GO Buses and GO Trains deliver passengers to these two locations separately.

[edit] Services

In addition to this, the Newmarket GO Station sells tickets exclusively on weekdays between the hours of 6:05am and 7:50am. This is the specific time period when the southbound Barrie - Bradford GO Trains reach Newmarket. The exception to this is on the last two work days of each month, when the station sells tickets between the hours of 17:50 and 19:15 to accommodate riders purchasing Monthly Passes for the following month. At other times of the day, the only other location to purchase GO bus or train tickets is at the GO Agency in the Newmarket Bus Terminal, which has much longer hours.
