Masuo Amada

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Masuo Amada (天田 益男, Amada Masuo?) (January 20, 1958 - ) is a male actor and seiyū from Hyōgo Prefecture. He is affiliated with the Seinenza Theater Company, and graduated from the Osaka University of Arts.
