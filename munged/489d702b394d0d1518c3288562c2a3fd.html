Lisa Yuen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lisa Yuen is a cast member of The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee on Broadway at the Circle in the Square Theatre. She is the understudy for Olive, Marcy, and Ms. Peretti. A native of Los Altos Hills, CA, and UCLA graduate, Lisa (affectionately known as "Coco") has also appeared in the Broadway and Paper Mill productions of Miss Saigon and the national touring companies of Flower Drum Song, The King and I, The Pirates of Penzance, and The Scarlet Pimpernel. Lisa is also featured in the newly released movie, World Trade Center, directed by Oliver Stone.

Lisa was recently featured as a guest on Martha Stewart, teaching Martha how to make realistic-looking flowers from paper.
