Black Tiger is an album by Y&T. Recorded at Ridge Farm, in Dorking, County of Surrey, England, it was released in 1982 on A&M Records.

  4. "Hell Or High Water"
  5. "Forever"
  6. "Black Tiger"
  7. "Barroom Boogie"
  8. "My Way Or The Highway"
  9. "Winds Of Change"
