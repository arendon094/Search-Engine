George Reiffenstein

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's Rowing
Silver 1904 St. Louis eights

George Patten "Pat" Reiffenstein (born March 23, 1883 - died ?) was a Canadian rower who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics.

In 1904 he was a member of Canadian boat, which won the silver medal in the eights.
