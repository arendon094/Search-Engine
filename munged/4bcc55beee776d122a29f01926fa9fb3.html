Tobatí District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tobatí is a district of the Cordillera Department, Paraguay.

Each year, a community service trip consisting of approximately 100 students from the Kingswood-Oxford School in West Hartford, Connecticut travel down to Tobatí, where one of the school's teachers has a mother who has a house in the town.

The school's "Team Tobati" (www.teamtobati.org) service organization sends a large group of students each year over spring break to Tobatí, Paraguay on a public service trip to work with rural villages in providing access to healthcare and education.
