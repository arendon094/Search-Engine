Filip Šitera (born April 18, 1988 in Mlada Boleslav, Czech Republic) is an international motorcycle speedway rider who has represented Czech Republic at Under-21 level. His uncles, Jan Verner and Vaclav Verner are both former Czech Republic international riders. His grandfather and manager Miloslav Verner also rode.

[edit] Brief career summary

Despite showing an interest to ride in the UK, and attracting the attention of several British clubs,[1] Šitera ruled out riding in the UK in 2008 due to racing and educational commitments but intends to ride there in 2009.[2] He already rides in Poland and signed a deal to ride in Sweden for Griparna in 2008.
