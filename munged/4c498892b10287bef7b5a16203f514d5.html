Cayuga Independent School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cayuga Independent School District is a public school district based in the community of Cayuga in unincorporated Anderson County, Texas (USA).

Located in northwestern Anderson County, the district has three campuses - Cayuga High (Grades 9-12), Cayuga Middle (Grades 6-8), and Cayuga Elementary (Grades PK-5) (2003 National Blue Ribbon School [1]).
