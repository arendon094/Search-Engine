Ulmanis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ulmanis is a surname that may refer to these Latvian politicians:

  1. Kārlis Ulmanis (1877–1942) — First Prime Minister of Latvia
  2. Guntis Ulmanis (1939) — President of Latvia 1993–1999; grandnephew of Kārlis Ulmanis
