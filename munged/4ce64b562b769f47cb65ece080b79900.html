Slaughter is an upcoming 2009 horror film which stars Erica Leerhsen, Shawnee Smith, and AnnaLynne McCord, and is directed by Víctor García.

[edit] Plot

Slaughter revolves around a young actress who travels to a remote location to shoot a film called "Slaughter", only to discover she and her co-stars have been cast in a Japanese snuff film and must fight to survive.

[edit] Trivia

  • Winner of the Slamdance Horror Screenplay Competition.
  • Dominique Swain was originally cast as Jenny, but had to drop out due to scheduling conflicts. Her role then went to Erica Leerhsen.
  • Maverick Films owes the original production crew for 2 weeks of work, yet is trying to continue making the film.
