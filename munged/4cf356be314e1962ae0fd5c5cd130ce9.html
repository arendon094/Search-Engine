Battle of the Nudes is the second solo album by Gordon Downie, lead singer of The Tragically Hip. It was released in 2003.

Downie's backing band on Coke Machine Glow was credited as "Country of Miracles". Participating musicians included Julie Doiron, Josh Finlayson and members of The Dinner Is Ruined.
