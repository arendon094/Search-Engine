Giorgos Bartzokas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Giorgos Bartzokas (Greek: Γιώργος Μπαρτζώκας) is a Greek basketball coach, currently coaching Olympia Larissas BC. He has been for many years assistant coach of Panagiotis Giannakis in Maroussi BC. In 2006 he signed as a head coach in Olympia Larissas BC. Along with Olympia, the same year, he managed to reach the play-offs of A1 Ethniki for the first time of its history, finishing 7th, and repeated the play-off qualification next year (2007/08).
