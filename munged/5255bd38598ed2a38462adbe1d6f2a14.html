Bălţăteşti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bălţăteşti is a commune situated 36 km north of Piatra Neamţ, on the road DN15C, in Neamţ County, Romania.

It has several sources of mineral waters used for disease treatment purposes, a hotel and medical facilities. The natural landscape and the surroundings makes it a touristic destination.

Coordinates: 47°07′N, 26°18′E
