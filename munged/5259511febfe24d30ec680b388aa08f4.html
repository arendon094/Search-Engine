7th Lok Sabha

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

List of Members of the 7th Lok Sabha, elected December 1979-January 1980. The Lok Sabha (House of the People) is the lower house in the Parliament of India.

[edit] Important members

  • Speaker:
    • Bal Ram Jakhar from 01-22-80 to 01-15-85
  • Deputy Speaker:
    • G. Lakshmanan from 02-01-80 to 12-31-84
  • Secretary General:
    • Avtar Singh Rikhy from 06-18-77 to 12-31-83
    • Subhash C Kashyap from 12-31-83 to 08-20-90
