Richard Ripley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men’s athletics
Bronze 1924 Paris 4 x 400 metre relay

Richard Nicholson Ripley (June 23, 1901 – July 1996) was a British athlete who competed mainly in the 400 metres.

He competed for Great Britain in the 1924 Summer Olympics held in Paris, France in the 4 x 400 metre relay where he won the bronze medal with his team mates Edward Toms, George Renwick and Guy Butler.
