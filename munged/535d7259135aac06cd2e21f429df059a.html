Colin Stack

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Colin Stack is a Boston composer and guitarist. Colin Stack's music can be described as a mix of jazz and pop blended with rich classical orchestrations. Stack is a founding member of the Socially Awkward Composers Ensemble in Boston. Colin Stack has collaborated with guitarist Chris Hersch, Harry Cherrin, Michael Mclaughlin, Michael Miller, New England Conservatory Orchestra, and Daniel Bennett.
