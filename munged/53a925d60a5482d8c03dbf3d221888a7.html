S. P. Sailaja

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

S.P. Sailaja
Origin Andhra Pradesh, India
Genre(s) playback singing, Indian classical
Occupation(s) Singer, Dubbing Artist
Years active 1978-2001

S.P. Sailaja, is a versatile Indian singer. She lent her voice to Telugu, Tamil and Kannada films. She has sung about 5000 songs to date.

[edit] Family

She was born as Sripathi Panditaradhyula Sailaja into an orthodox Saivite family in Konetammapeta in the Nellore district of Andhra Pradesh . Her father SP Sambamurthy was a well-known exponent of Harikatha and her brother SP Balasubramanyam is a prolific singer in Telugu, Tamil, Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam and many other languages. She is married to Subhalekha Sudhakar, a Tollywood Actor. The couple have a son, Sreekar.

[edit] Dubbing artist

She has also given voice for various artists like Tabu, Sonali Bendre etc.

[edit] Actress

She acted in Sagara Sangamam, a Telugu film directed by K. Viswanath in which she played the role of a Classical dancer.

[edit] Other Facts

Besides a Playback singer, she is also a Classical Dancer.
