Piano Seven

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Originally conceived by pianists François Lindemann and Sebastian Santa Maria in 1987 as a one-off experiment for seven pianists playing together, Piano Seven enjoyed such success that the concert had a second run and the Swiss-based ensemble was soon born. This Ensemble of seven pianists have played in numerous Opera Houses and classical halls around Europe (Switzerland, France, Belgium) Liban, Egypt, Brasil and Asia (Thailand, Hong Kong, Singapore, Taiwan and China).
