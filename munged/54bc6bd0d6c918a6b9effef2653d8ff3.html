Frome Sports Club

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Frome Sports Club is a multi-use stadium in Savanna-la-Mar, Jamaica. It is currently used mostly for football matches. It serves as a home ground of Reno F.C.. The stadium holds 2,000 people.
