The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Goiânia {Latin: Goianien(sis)} is an archdiocese located in the city of Goiânia in Brazil.

[edit] History

  • March 26, 1956: Established as Metropolitan Archdiocese of Goiânia from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Goiás

[edit] Special Churches

  • Minor Basillicas:
    • Basílica do Divino Pai Eterno, Trindade

[edit] Leadership

  • Archbishops of Goiânia (Roman rite)
    • Archbishop Washington Cruz, C.P. (2002.05.08 – present)
    • Archbishop Antônio Ribeiro de Oliveira (1985.10.23 – 2002.05.08)
    • Archbishop Fernando Gomes dos Santos (1957.03.07 – 1985.06.01)

  • Goiás
  • Ipameri
  • Itumbiara
