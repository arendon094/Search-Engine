Uppuguda is one of the old suburbs in Hyderabad, India. It is part of the old city of Hyderabad.

[edit] Commercial area

The choice for shops at Uppuguda is very good.

[edit] Public Transport

Uppuguda is connected by buses run by APSRTC, since a Bus Depot is close by, it is well connected. Buses that run are 89.

There is a MMTS train station is at Uppuguda.
