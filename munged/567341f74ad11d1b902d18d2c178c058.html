Futatsui Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Futatsui Eki

Futatsui Station (二ツ井駅, Futatsui Eki?) is a railway station on the JR East Ōu Main Line. It is located in Noshiro (formerly Futatsui), Akita, Japan.

[edit] Station structure

  • 2-way, 3-track, above-ground station.
Platforms
1 ■Ōu Main Line for Higashi-Noshiro and Akita
2 ■Ōu Main Line for Ōdate and Aomori
3 ■Ōu Main Line (siding)
Note: Track 3 is used primarily for freight trains changing direction.
  • Futatsui Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Higashi-Noshiro Station, and operated by JR Akita total service. Ordinary tickets, express tickets, and reserved-seat tickets for all JR lines are on sale.
    • Business hours: 6:45 a.m. - 5:50 p.m.

[edit] Usage statistics

Averaged 474 passengers per day during 2005.

  • Futatsui Post Office (delivery and collection office)
