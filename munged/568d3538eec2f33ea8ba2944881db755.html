Antonio Pérez Delgadillo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Pérez Delgadillo (born April 16, 1978 in Guadalajara, Jalisco) is a Mexican football goalkeeper who currently plays for León in the Mexican First Division A.

[edit] Trivia

He is nicknamed 'El Niño Gerber' by Univision commentator Pablo Ramirez because he resembles the baby on the baby food label.
