Ancinnes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ancinnes is a commune located in the Sarthe département, in the Pays de la Loire région.

It is 8 km from Alençon, the capital of the Orne department and the nearest major town. The commune of Ancinnes has approximately 1,100 residents. The village is centred on the Church of Saint Peter and Saint Paul.
