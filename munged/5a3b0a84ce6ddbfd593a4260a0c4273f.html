Jatin Bora

Jatin Bora is an Indian actor who has appeared in Assamese language movies. He had a minuscule role in Kolahal, his first professional film.

His official debut movie was Uttarkaal, opposite Moyola Goswami. The movie was successful and Jatin became a Superstar of Assamese cinema. He has also appeared in the movie Kanyadaan, opposite Divya Desai, Mridula Baruah and Chetana Das.

He started his directorial venture in 2006 with a film named 'Adhinayak' opposite Nichita Goswami.

He is also actively involved in Assamese Theatre Industry.
