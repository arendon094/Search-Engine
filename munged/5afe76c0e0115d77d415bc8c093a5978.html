Alive Alive-O
Live album by The Dubliners
Released 1997
Recorded 1996
Genre Irish folk
The Dubliners chronology
Further Along Alive Alive-O The Definitive Transatlantic Collection

This live double album was recorded in December 1996 in Germany at the end of The Dubliners' European tour. John Sheahan's daughter Ceoladh guests with her father, duetting on fiddle with him on his composition, "Among Friends".
