Jasey-Jay Anderson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jasey-Jay Anderson is a Canadian snowboarder who competed in the 2006 Winter Olympics.

In the Men’s Giant Parallel Slalom event he finished in 20th place[1], but came in 5th in the Snowboard Men’s Cross. [2]
