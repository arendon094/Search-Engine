Deck the Halls (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The phrase Deck the Halls can refer to:

  • the Christmas carol "Deck the Halls"
  • the 2003 mystery novel Deck the Halls by Mary Higgins Clark and Carol Higgins Clark
  • the 2006 film Deck the Halls starring Danny DeVito and Matthew Broderick
  • The Relient K Christmas CD Deck the Halls, Bruise Your Hand
  • the 2005 made-for-television Christmas film Deck the Halls starring Gabrielle Carteris and Steve Bacic.
