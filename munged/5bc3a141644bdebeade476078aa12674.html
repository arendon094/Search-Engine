Gare de Surgères

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Surgères is a railway station in Surgères, France. It is served by both TER Poitou-Charentes and TGV:

  • La Rochelle - Gare Montparnasse (TGV Atlantique)
  • La Rochelle - Poitiers (TER Poitou-Charentes)
