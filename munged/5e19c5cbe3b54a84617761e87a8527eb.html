Solson Publications

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Solson Publications was a New York publisher of a number of black-and-white comics in the 1980s, including:

Reagan's Raiders: A Typical Solson Product

Cover to Reagan's Raiders #1
Publisher Solson Publications, Inc
Schedule Monthly
Publication date 1986
Number of issues 3
Main character(s) Ronald Reagan, Caspar Weinberger
Creative team
Creator(s) Monroe Arnold, Rich Buckler
  • Amazing Wahzoo ("Wha-Whoom!")
  • The Bushido Blade of Zatoichi Walrus
  • Codename: Ninja ("Martial arts drama so real you'd swear it was a movie")
  • Daffy Qadaffi[1]
  • Iron Maidens ("Loads of musclebound fun")
  • Reagan's Raiders[2] ("The Ultimate Patriotic Super Heroes Book"), co-created by Monroe Arnold and Solson's editor Rich Buckler
  • Samurai Santa
  • Sultry Teenage Super-Foxes (drawn by Chuck Wotjkiewicz)

The company was founded by Gary Brodsky, son of Sol Brodsky (Sol's son = Solson).
