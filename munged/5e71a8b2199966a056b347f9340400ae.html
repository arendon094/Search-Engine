Saskatchewan Highway 397

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Highway 397 is a highway in the Canadian province of Saskatchewan. It runs from Highway 763/Highway 764 at Allan to Highway 16. Highway 397 is about 12 km (7 mi.) long.

Highway 397 begins at Allan and runs northward concurrently with Highway 763 until an intersection 5 km north of Allan, where Highway 763 takes a different route. Highway 397 ends 7 km later at Highway 16.
