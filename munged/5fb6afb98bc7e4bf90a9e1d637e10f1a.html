Lost Souls

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lost Souls may refer to:

  • Lost Souls (film), a 2000 movie directed by Janusz Kamiński and starring Winona Ryder and Ben Chaplin
  • Lost Souls (1998 TV-Movie), a Mystery/Thriller UPN original movie directed by Jeff Woolnough, starring John Savage and Barbara Sukowa.
  • "Lost Souls" is a 1997 miniseries created by Matthew Schutt
  • Lost Souls (novel), the 1992 debut novel of horror author Poppy Z. Brite
  • Lost Souls (album), the 2000 debut album by British band Doves
    • Lost Souls, the name of the title track from the above album
  • Earth 2150: Lost Souls, an expansion to the computer game, Earth 2150
  • Lost Souls (1980 film), a Chinese film
