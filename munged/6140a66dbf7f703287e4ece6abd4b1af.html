Margaret Crowley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Margaret Crowley (born 1986-03-17 in St. Paul, Minnesota) is an Olympic speed skater from the United States, who competed in 3000 m and the team pursuit at the 2006 Winter Olympics.
