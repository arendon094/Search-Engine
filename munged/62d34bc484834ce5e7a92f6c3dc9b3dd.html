Martin Branner

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Martin Branner was a cartoonist probably best known for his comic strip Winnie Winkle, which he produced from 1920 to 1962. He also produced the strip Looie from 1938 through 1960. He received the National Cartoonist Society Humor Comic Strip Award for it in 1958.
