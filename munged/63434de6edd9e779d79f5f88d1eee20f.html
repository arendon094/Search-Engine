◄ Work Your Magic (2007)   

"Hasta La Vista" is a English language song (though the title is in Spanish and a Russian version has also been recorded), by Belarusian Ruslan Alekhno. The song was performed as the Belarusian entry in the Eurovision Song Contest 2008. The song was originally written in a dance-pop style, but has been heavily re-arranged and remixed for the contest in a pop-rock style more similar to the 2007 Belarusian Eurovision entry: Work Your Magic.
