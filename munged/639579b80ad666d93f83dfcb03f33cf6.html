The Flightstar Group, Inc. is a Fort Lauderdale, Florida based diversified international aviation company and its executive, operations and sales offices are located nearby Fort Lauderdale Executive Airport (ICAO code KFXE / IATA FXE).

Flightstar Group is experienced and it specializes in the trading, sales, remarketing, financing and leasing of various types of aircraft, with a proven track record of closed transaction. Flightstar Group engages in cross-border transactions on the sale, lease or financing of used or surplus commercial jet, regional jet and turboprop aircraft and VIP executive aircraft, including turoprop and turobfan jet engines.

The Flightstar Group, Inc. specializes in L-1011 including Boeing 727, B737, Boeing 747, B757, , B767, Douglas DC-10, Airbus A310, A320, A330 and A340. Commuter / Regional Aircraft include the DeHavilland Dash 8, Twin Otter, ATR 42 and ATR 72, Canadair RJ, Beechcraft King Air, Learjet, Hawker HS-125 and the BAE Jetstream J31 and J32 aircraft. The company manages or holds title to several jet and cabin class aircraft.

Flightstar Group maintains trading partners and agents worldwide, including offices in Paraguay, Colombia, Peru, Paraguay, Brazil and Mexico in the Americas, with agents in Belgium, South Africa, Jordan, UAE and Malaysia.

The company also provides arrangements for Large Transport Aircraft Charters (passenger or cargo) in conjunction with several international aircraft operators to provide ad-hock charters, sports charters and ACMI wet-lease services to airlines, corporations, sports teams, entertainment industry and/or tour operators worldwide.

Affiliate: Flightstar Air Express, Inc. (dba Flightstar Express) is a soon to be FAA Part 135 Certified Air Carrier / Air Taxi subject to United States FAA Approval. The company will initially fly the Piper T-1020 airliner.

The Flightstar Group, Inc. is a State of Florida Corporation, it was established in 1993 and has completed more than 70 aircraft sales, leases or financing since it started. The company CEO and president is Mr. John Poindexter, CFO & VP of Capital Markets is Tatiana Cortez, VP of Technical Sales, Dio Santos, Director of Sales for Latin America, Narcisco Camargo, Director of Maintenance is David Bettencourt.
