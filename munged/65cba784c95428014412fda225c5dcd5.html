PPS-1350

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Two Snecma PPS 1350 at the Paris Air Show 2007

PPS-1350 is a Hall effect thruster, a kind of ion propulsion system for spacecraft. It was used in the SMART-1 mission to the moon. It creates a stream of electrically charged ions. The PPS-1350 was created by Snecma, a French aerospace firm.
