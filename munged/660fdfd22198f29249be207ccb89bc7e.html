"To Sheila" is the first track on The Smashing Pumpkins' 1998 album Adore. Although only two commercial singles emerged from Adore, both "Crestfallen" and "To Sheila" received promotional releases.[1]

"To Sheila" was also featured in The O.C. episode "The Debut".
