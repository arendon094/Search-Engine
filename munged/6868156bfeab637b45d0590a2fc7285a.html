Leon Stickle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Leon Stickle (born April 20, 1948 in Toronto, Ontario) is a retired National Hockey League linesman. His career started in 1969 and ended in 1997. During his career (in which he wore a helmet from the mid-1980s until his retirement), he officiated six Stanley Cup finals, 1,987 regular season games, and four All-Star games.
