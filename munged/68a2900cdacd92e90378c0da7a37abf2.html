Stadionul Dinamo (Chişinău)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Stadionul Dinamo is a multi-use stadium in Chişinău, Moldova. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of FC Politehnica Chişinău. The stadium holds 2,692 people.
