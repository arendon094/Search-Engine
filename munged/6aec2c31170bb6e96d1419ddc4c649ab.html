Joost Scholte (born October 27, 1978) is a Dutch film producer and actor.

[edit] Biography

Scholte was born in Bergen op Zoom, Noord-Brabant, the Netherlands; to producer Sjef Scholte who was the producer of Kameleon 1 and 2. Scholte was an only child.

Approximately Scholte's height is 6 foot and 6 inches (translated: 1.98m).

Scholte started his career as the "Miscellaneous Crew" member, and did the films, The Mechanik and The Black Dahlia. After he became a film actor, appearing in the films War, Inc., and also The Black Dahlia, then after became a film producer, producing the films, Wicked Little Things, and the upcoming horror remake Day of the Dead.

Currently Scholte is in assistant line-producing the new remake horror film Day of the Dead.
