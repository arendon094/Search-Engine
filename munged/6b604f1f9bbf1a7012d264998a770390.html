Carl Kolling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Carl Kölling (1831-1914) was a German composer of piano music.

Two works available for the intermediate piano student are (Lose Blätter) "Fluttering Leaves in C Major" Op. 147, No. 1 and "Fluttering Leaves in A Minor" Op. 147, No. 2 found in "Masterpieces with Flair" published by Alfred Publishing Company, Inc. "Hungary (Rapsodie Mignonne)" by Carl Koelling, Op. 410 is in "Your Favorite Solos for Piano" compiled and edited by George Walter Anthony from Th. Presser.
