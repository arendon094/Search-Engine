Phaulostephanus
Fossil range: Jurassic[1]
Scientific classification
Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Mollusca
Class: Cephalopoda
Subclass: Ammonoidea
Genus: Phaulostephanus

Phaulostephanus is an extinct genus from a well known class of fossil cephalopods, the ammonites. It lived during the Jurassic Period[1], which lasted from approximately 200 to 145 million years ago.
