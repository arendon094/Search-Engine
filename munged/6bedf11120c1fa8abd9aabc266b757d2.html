Chornomorske raion (Ukrainian: Чорноморський район, Russian: Черноморский район, Crimean Tatar: Aqmeçit rayonı) is one of the 25 regions of Crimea. It is located in the far west of the republic on the Tarhanqut peninsula. The raion's centre is the town of Chornomorske.
