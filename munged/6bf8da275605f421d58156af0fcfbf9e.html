1966 and All That

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

1966 and All That (based on the title of the 1930 book 1066 and All That by W. C. Sellar and R. J. Yeatman) is the title of:

  • 1966 and All That (book) (ISBN 0340897112), a 2005 book by British satirist Craig Brown, in the same style as 1066 and All That
  • 1966 and All That (radio), radio adaptation of the above book
  • 1966 and All That, a 1970 book on British economics, by D. S. Lees (ISBN 0900572167)
  • 1966 and All That, a 1986 art exhibit at the Whitworth Art Gallery (ISBN 0862940877 for the exhibit catalog)
  • 1966 and All That, a 2001 autobiography of soccer player Geoff Hurst (ISBN 0747241872)
  • 1966 and All That, a 1969 treatise on the eucharist in Church of England liturgy
  • 1966 and All That, a song by Half Man Half Biscuit on the CD (PROBE4), 1988 (retitled Back In The D.H.S.S./The Trumpton Riots EP).
