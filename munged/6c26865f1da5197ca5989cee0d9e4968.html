Westmount—Saint-Louis is a provincial electoral district in Quebec, Canada that elects members to the National Assembly of Quebec. The riding was created in 1992 from parts of Saint-Louis, Westmount and Sainte-Anne. It includes mostly portions of the demerged city of Westmount as well as most of the Ville-Marie borough and downtown area of Montreal.

[edit] Members of the National Assembly

  1. Jacques Chagnon, Liberal (1994-present)
