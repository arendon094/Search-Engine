SMS Emden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

SMS Emden may refer to one of the following ships of the German Kaiserliche Marine and named after the town of Emden on the Ems River:

  • SMS Emden (1906), a Dresden-class light cruiser commissioned in 1909. She sank or captured nearly thirty Allied merchant vessels during World War I, and was scuttled after the Battle of Cocos in 1914
  • SMS Emden (1915), a Königsberg-class cruiser launched in 1915 and scuttled at Scapa Flow in 1919
