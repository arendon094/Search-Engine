Måns Herngren

Måns Herngren, born on April 20, 1965, in Stockholm, is a Swedish actor, but mostly a director. Almost all of his films are done together with his old friend Hannes Holm. He is the older brother to actor and comedian Felix Herngren.

Måns Herngren was married to Lena Philipsson from 1993 to 2002. He is now married to former high jumper Kajsa Bergqvist.

[edit] Selected filmography

  • 1990: S*M*A*S*H (Director)
  • 1995: En på miljonen (One In A Million) (Director)
  • 1997: Adam & Eva (Director)
  • 2000: Det blir aldrig som man tänkt sig (Things Never End Up The Way You've Planned) (Director)
  • 2002: Klassfesten (The Class Reunion) (Director)
  • 2006: Varannan vecka (Every Other Week) (Actor and director)
