Friedrich Bödicker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Vizeadmiral Friedrich Bödicker, (13 March 1866 – 20 September 1944) was a flag officer of the Kaiserliche Marine during the First World War. He is perhaps best known for being present at the Battle of Jutland, where ships of his Second Scouting Group fired some of the first shots of the action.
