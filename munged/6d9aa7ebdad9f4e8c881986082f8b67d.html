Sonet Film

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sonet Film is a Swedish film production company.

Established in 1984, Sonet Film is one of Scandinavia's leading film companies and distributors of cinema. Since 1997 it is owned by the media company MTG Modern Times Group.

The company has produced many films by the Swedish filmmaker Lukas Moodysson.
