[edit] Sports

  • In baseball, the Atlanta Braves use the tomahawk as the weapon of choice. At times, when excitement is high, fans use foam tomahawks, or simply their hands, to do a chopping motion referred to as the "tomahawk chop".
  • In basketball, a tomahawk is a one-handed slam dunk, where the ball is brought over the head before being stuffed into the basket, in a chopping motion
  • A turning maneuver used in inline skating
  • The United States national rugby league team, nicknamed the Tomahawks
  • The mascot for the P.E.I. Rocket of the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League
  • In field hockey, a tomahawk is a type of hit where the hockey stick is turned so that the rounded side faces the ground and then swung in such a way that it hits the ball in a downward chopping motion.
  • At Algonquin Regional High School in Northborough, MA the Tomahawk is the school's mascot
  • In skiing a Tomahawk is when you flip head over heals down the mountain.
