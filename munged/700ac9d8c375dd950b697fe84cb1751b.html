Nadăş River (Mureş)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nadăş River
Countries Romania
Counties Arad County
Mouth Mureş

The Nadăş River (Hungarian: Nádas) is a tributary of the Mureş River in Romania. The name in Hungarian means "reedy". The Romanian name derives from that.
