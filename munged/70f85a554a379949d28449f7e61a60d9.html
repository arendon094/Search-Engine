Massa e Cozzile is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Pistoia in the Italian region Tuscany, located about 45 km northwest of Florence and about 14 km west of Pistoia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 7,502 and an area of 16.0 km².[1]

The municipality of Massa e Cozzile contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Croci, Cozzile, Margine Coperta, Massa, Traversagna, and Vangile.

Massa e Cozzile borders the following municipalities: Buggiano, Marliana, Montecatini-Terme, Pescia.
