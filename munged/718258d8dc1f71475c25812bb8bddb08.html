Blue angelfish

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blue angelfish can refer to either of two species of marine angelfish in the genus Holacanthus:

  • Holacanthus bermudensis, the Bermuda blue angelfish.
  • Holacanthus isabelita, the Blue angelfish.
