Selwyn Jalil Lymon (born September 21, 1986 in Fort Wayne, Indiana) is an American football wide receiver for the Miami Dolphins of the National Football League. He was originally signed by the Dolphins as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Purdue.

[edit] Personal

Lymon is a cousin of NFL safety Bernard Pollard.
