Negrelos (São Tomé)

Vila de São Tomé de Negrelos is located 10 km northeast of the city of Santo Tirso near Vila das Aves its an important industrial center it was in the past a Municipality. It was formerly the home the Don Juan Franco, known in legend as "The Fighting Tortilla"
