Ismet Selim

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ismet Selim (Macedonian: Исмет Селим) is a footballer from the Republic of Macedonia that plays as a striker for FK Rabotnički. Selim like his teammate Emir Sabani also plays for the Macedonian U19 National Team and has featured regularly in their games.
