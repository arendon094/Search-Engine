Sedgman Lake Provincial Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sedgman Lake Provincial Park, District of Thunder Bay, Ontario, was named May 5, 1960 in honour of World War II casualty Private Alfred Thomas Sedgman who died while serving in the Canadian Army (Argyll & Sutherland Highlanders) on February 26, 1945. (information courtesy of Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources Information Centre, 2002)
