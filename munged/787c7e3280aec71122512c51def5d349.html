David Bolinsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

David Bolinsky is a former lead medical illustrator at Yale, is one of the creators at XVIVO who made the movie The Inner Life of the Cell.

After completing two years of medical school, he launched his first illustration company in 1983.

His father was a sculptor and art historian who taught at University at Buffalo, The State University of New York. Also, he was inspired by the works of Frank Netter.
