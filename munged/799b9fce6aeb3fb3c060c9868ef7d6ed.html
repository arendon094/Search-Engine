Camino Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Camino Records is an independent record label, founded by English guitarist/songwriter Steve Hackett. The label was originally set up as a way for Hackett to re-release selections from his solo career, but in recent years, Hackett has also released new albums on the label.
