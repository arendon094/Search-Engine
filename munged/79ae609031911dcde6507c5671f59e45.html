A killjoy is a person who is anti-fun, or prevents others from having fun.

Killjoy may refer to:

In music:

  • Killjoy (album), an album by Shihad
  • Killjoy (musician), an American musician with the bands Necrophagia and Viking Crown
  • The Killjoys, a 1980s Australian band
  • The Killjoys (Canadian band), a 1990s Canadian band
  • "The Killjoy", a song by Insomnium from Above the Weeping World

In other uses:

  • Killjoy (1981 film), a made-for-television movie starring Kim Basinger
  • Killjoy (2000 film), a horror film written by Carl Washington
  • Killjoy (comics), a fictional character created by Steve Ditko
  • Killjoy (a.k.a. Killspree), a fictional character from the Marvel Comics series Weapon P.R.I.M.E.
  • Dr. Killjoy, a fictional character in the video game The Suffering
