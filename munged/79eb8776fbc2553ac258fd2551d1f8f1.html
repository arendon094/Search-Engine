Zürich Hardbrücke

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Zürich Hardbrücke" label on the platform

Zürich Hardbrücke is a train station on the Zürich S-Bahn system in the central part of the Swiss city of Zürich. The station was opened in 1982 and expanded in 1990 in line with the introduction of the S-Bahn scheme.
