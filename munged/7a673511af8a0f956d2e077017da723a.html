USS Chinook (PC-9) was the ninth Cyclone class Patrol (coastal) ship. Awarded July 19, 1991 to Bollinger Machine, her keel was laid June 16, 1993 and she was launched February 26, 1994. She was delivered October 7, 1994 and commissioned January 28, 1995.
