KZEN (100.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music format.[1] Licensed to Central City, Nebraska, USA, the station serves the Columbus and Grand Island areas. The station is currently owned by Three Eagles Communications and features programing from ABC Radio .[2]
