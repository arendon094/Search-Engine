Shen Po-tsang (traditional Chinese: 沈柏蒼) born August 31, 1977 in Taiwan, is a Taiwanese baseball player who currently plays for Uni-President Lions of Chinese Professional Baseball League. He currently plays as short reliever for the Lions.
