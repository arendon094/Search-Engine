Daniel Riordan born 25 September 1984 in Galway, Connacht, Ireland is a rugby union player for Connacht Rugby in the Magners League.

He is also contracted to Buccaneers RFC who play in AIB League Division 2.

Daniel Riordan's position of choice is as a Full-back and he can also operate as a Centre or on the Wing.

Riordan was called into the Ireland A side that was defeated by England Saxons on 1 February 2008. [1] [2]
