Relámpago (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Relámpago (or El Relámpago, Relampago) is the Spanish word for lightning.

Relámpago may also refer to:

  • El Relampago, one of the many pseudonymns of Cuban-wrestler and rapper of Puerto Rican descent, Charles Ashenoff
  • Relampago, a comic book character created by Judge Margarito Garza and currently published under Azteca Productions
  • Relámpago Vivo, the spanish-translation and alternate alias of the comic book superhero, Living Lightning
  • Los Relámpagos del Norte, a Mexican norteño band
  • Relampago, Texas
  • Relámpago, the legendary white steed of Emiliano Zapata
This disambiguation page lists articles associated with the same title. If an internal link led you here, you may wish to change the link to point directly to the intended article.
