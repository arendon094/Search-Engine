Roy Sentjens (born December 15, 1980 in Neerpelt) is a Belgian professional road bicycle racer for UCI ProTeam Predictor-Lotto. His major victory to date was in winning the Belgian semi-classic Kuurne-Brussels-Kuurne in 2003. Sentjens started his career with Rabobank in 2002, and transferred to Predictor-Lotto for the 2007 season. Sentjens holds dual nationality of Belgian and Dutch. From 2002 until 2004 he raced under a Dutch licence. However, in 2005, he changed to a Belgian licence as he felt more Belgian than Dutch.
