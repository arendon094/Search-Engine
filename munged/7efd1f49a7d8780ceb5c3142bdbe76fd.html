[edit] Asia

  • Ani - Gagik of Ani, King of Ani (989-1020)
  • Byzantine Empire - Basil II Bulgaroktonus, Byzantine emperor (976-1025)
  • Caliphate(Abbasid) - Al-Qadir, Caliph in Baghdad (991-1031)
  • China (Northern Song Dynasty) - Zhenzong, Emperor of Song China (997-1022)
  • Ghaznavid Empire - Mahmud of Ghaznavid (998-1030)
  • Japan -
    • Monarch - Emperor Ichijō, emperor of Japan (986-1011)
    • de facto - Fujiwara no Michinaga (995-1017)
  • Khitan Empire - Shengzong, Khitan Emperor (982-1031)
  • Khmer Empire - Jayaviravarman (1002-1011) & Suryavarman I (1001-1050)
  • Korea (Goryeo Dynasty) - Mokjong, King of Goryeo (997-1009)
  • Tamil Nadu - Rajaraja Chola, King of Tamil Nadu (985-1014)

[edit] Europe

  • Bulgaria - Samuil, Tsar of Bulgaria (976-1014)
  • Byzantine Empire - Basil II Bulgaroktonus, Byzantine emperor (976-1025)
  • Denmark - Sweyn I of Denmark, also known as Sweyn Forkbeard, King of Denmark (985 - February 2, 1014); also King of Norway
  • England - Ethelred II, also known as Ethelred the Unready, King of England (March 18, 978 - December 25, 1013 and February 2, 1014 - April 23, 1016)
  • France - Robert II, King of France (996-1031)
  • Holy Roman Empire (Germany) - Henry II, Holy Roman Emperor of the Ottonian dynasty (elected and coronated June 7, 1002, becoming de facto Emperor; later crowned by Pope Benedict VIII on February 14, 1014, died July 13, 1024). Also King of Italy.
  • Hungary - Stephen I, King of Hungary (997-1038)
  • Ireland - Brian Boru, High King of Ireland (1002 - 1014)
  • Norway - Sweyn I of Denmark, King of Norway (999 - February 2, 1014); also King of Denmark
  • Poland - Bolesław I the Brave, Duke of Poland (992-1025)
  • Scotland - Malcolm II, King of Scots (1005-1034)
  • Spain - Hisham II of the Umayyad Dynasty, Caliph of Cordoba (976-1008 and 1010-1012)
  • Sweden - Olof the Tax-king, King of Sweden (995-1022)
  • Wales (List of rulers of Wales)
    • Gwynedd - Llywelyn ap Seisyll, Prince of Gwynedd (1003-1023)
  • Venice - Pietro II Orseolo, Doge of Venice (991-1009)
