Grady Lewis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Grady Lewis (born March 25, 1917 in Boyd, Texas) was an American professional basketball player.

A 6'7" forward/center from the University of Oklahoma, Lewis played three seasons (1946-1949) in the Basketball Association of America as a member of the Detroit Falcons, St. Louis Bombers, and Baltimore Bullets. He averaged 5.4 points per game in his career and won a league championship with Baltimore in 1948.
