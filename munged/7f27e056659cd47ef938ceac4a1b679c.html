Call-Me-Kevin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Call-Me-Kevin is a fictional kaiju from the live-action show Kaiju Big Battel. He is an alien that lost his memory on the way to Earth.

[edit] Finishing and signature moves

  • Deep Sea Dive


[edit] The Sea Amigos

Unibouzu leads a Rogue stable with two other Kaiju: D.W. Cycloptopuss III and outer space sea beast Call-Me-Kevin.

[edit] Funfacts

  • Call-Me-Kevin has never won a battle: even the announcer Louden Noxious (as a last second replacement for perennial loser Super Wrong) has defeated him. Call-Me-Kevin had been the perennial loser in Kaiju Big Battel until on November 9, 2007 at "More Better Fighto!" in the Warsaw, Call-Me-Kevin finally gains victory over Uchu Chu and is now the current KBB Champion.
