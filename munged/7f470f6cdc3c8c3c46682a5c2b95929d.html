Zelda (given name)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Zelda is a given name meaning "female warrior". And a nickname for the feminine name Griselda which means "dark battle".

[edit] People

  • Zelda Fitzgerald (July 24, 1900—March 10, 1948), born Zelda Sayre, the wife of writer F. Scott Fitzgerald.
  • Zelda Schneersohn Mishkovsky (1914—1984), (pen-name "Zelda"), Israeli poet, notable writer of Hebrew religious poetry.
  • Zelda Harris (born February 17, 1985), an American actor.
  • Zelda La Grange (born October 29th, 1970), former private secretary to President Nelson Mandela.
  • Zelda Rubinstein, notable for starring in the Poltergeist film series.
  • Zelda Williams, an American actress and daughter of Robin Williams.
  • Zelda Zonk, a pseudonym used by Marilyn Monroe (1926—1962).

[edit] Uses in fiction

  • Princess Zelda, a character in The Legend of Zelda video game series.
  • Zelda Gilroy, a character in the 1959—1963 TV series, The Many Loves of Dobie Gillis.
  • Zelda, Sabrina's aunt in Sabrina, the Teenage Witch.
  • Zelda, a character in the Gerry Anderson TV series Terrahawks.
  • Zelda, a character in the serial Galaxy Four from the BBC television series Doctor Who.
  • Zelda, the dog companion in Nickelodeon magazine.
  • Zelda, the maid of Katinka van de Velde, a Dutch Governor's adulterous wife, in Wilbur Smith's Birds of Prey.
