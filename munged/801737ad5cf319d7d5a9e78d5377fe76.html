CineVox

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the film production company. For the Italian record label, see Cinevox.

CineVox Filmproduktion GmbH is a film and television production company based in Germany. It was founded in 1983 and went all the way until its closedown in 1999.

[edit] Films and series

  • The NeverEnding Story II: The Next Chapter
  • Knight Moves
  • Benefit of the Doubt
  • The NeverEnding Story III
  • The Neverending Story (co-production with Ellipse and Nelvana)
  • The House with a Clock in Its Walls
