Rita Barberá Nolla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rita Barberá Nolla is the mayor of Valencia, Spain from 1991. Member of the National Council of the People's Party of Spain and Representant in the Valencian regional Parliament (Corts Valencianes).
