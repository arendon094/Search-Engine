Philadelphia Céilí Group

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Philadelphia Portal

The Philadelphia Céilí Group is a music organization in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, known for an annual Philadelphia Céilí Festival. The Group claims that its festival is the only in the area that is "truly a traditional festival" and it is "one of the oldest continuous (if not the oldest) traditional Irish music and dance" festivals in the United States. The Group was founded in 1958.
