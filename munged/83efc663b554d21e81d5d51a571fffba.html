Pierre Maille

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pierre Maille, French politician born on 14 June 1947 in Frejus Former Mayor of Brest from 1982 to 1983, then from 1989 to 2001. He has succeeded to Francis Le Ble who died during his mandate. He belongs to the French Socialist Party. Since 1998, he leads the general council of department of Finistere.
