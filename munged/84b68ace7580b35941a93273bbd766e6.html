2919 Dali

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

2919 Dali is a small main belt asteroid, which was discovered by Schelte J. Bus in 1981. It is named after Salvador Dalí, the Spanish surrealist artist. 2919 Dali has a period (of rotation round the sun) of 5 years 218 days.
