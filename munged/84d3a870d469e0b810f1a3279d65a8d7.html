Oedipus Hex is the fifth episode in the third season of the popular American crime drama CSI: NY. The main case centers around the death of a member of the Suicide Girls performance group.

[edit] See also

  • CSI: NY
  • List of CSI: NY episodes

This episode offers one of the first glimpses into the trauma suffered some years previous by Lindsay Monroe, who was the lone survivor of a robbery-homicide that took place in Montana. A number of her friends were killed, while she escaped with her life.
