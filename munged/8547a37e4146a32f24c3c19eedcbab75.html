Jayne Tunnicliffe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jayne Bickerton (nee Tunnicliffe born 1967 in Bradford, West Yorkshire, England) is an English actress.

She played recurring character Yana Lumb in Coronation Street between 2005 and 2007.
