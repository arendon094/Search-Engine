Emanuel Giani Ruset

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Emanuel or Manolache Giani Ruset was a Prince of Wallachia (May 1770 - October 1771), and Prince of Moldavia (May 11, 1788 - October 1788). He was a Phanariote and member of the Rosetti family.
