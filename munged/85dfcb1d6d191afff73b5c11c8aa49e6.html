Lars Øyno

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lars Øyno (b. 1955) is a Norwegian actor and director who lives and works in Oslo.

Øyno's theatrical ideology is greatly inspired by the visions of Antonin Artaud. He manages the underground theatre Grusomhetens Teater (Theatre of Cruelty) at Hausmania.

Productions by Grusomhetens Teater include:

  • Thomasevangeliet (2005)
  • Peer Gynt
  • Alaska

Øyno has portrayed his countryman Knut Hamsun in the eponymous Norwegian mini-series.
