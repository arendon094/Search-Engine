Bogd Khan Uul

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bogd Khan Uul (Mongolian Богд хан уул) is a mountain in Mongolia that overlooks the nation's capital, Ulaanbaatar, from a height of 3000 feet (914m) above the city.
