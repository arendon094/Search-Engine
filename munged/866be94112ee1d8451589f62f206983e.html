Dries Devenyns (born July 22, 1983) is a Belgian professional road bicycle racer for UCI ProTeam Predictor-Lotto.

[edit] Palmares

  •  Belgium Amateur Time Trial Champion (2006)
  • Ruban Granitier Breton - 2 stages & Overall (2006)
  • Tour des Pyrénées - 1 stage (2006)
  • 2nd, National Amateur Road Race Championship (2006)
  •  Belgium U23 Road Race Champion (2005)
  • 3rd, National U17 Cyclo-Cross Championship (1999)
