Daisy Rast

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Daisy Rast is one of the top fashion photographers in Los Angeles California and also a photographic artist exhibiting work at numerous galleries including Create Fixate around the Southwest United States. Her work can be seen in numerous publications and she can be found shooting for several different clothing labels including Splendid and Ella Moss.
