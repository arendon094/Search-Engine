Bryan Foy (8 December 1896 â€“ 20 April 1977), was an American film producer and director. He produced 214 films between 1924 and 1963. He also directed 41 films between 1923 and 1934.

He was born in Chicago, Illinois and died in Los Angeles, California from a heart attack.
