Sens-de-Bretagne (Breton: Sen) is a French commune located in the Ille-et-Vilaine département, in the Bretagne région.

[edit] Demographics

Inhabitants of Sens-de-Bretagne are called Senonais.

As of the census of 1999, the village had a population of 1,515.
The estimation for 2004 was 1,789.
