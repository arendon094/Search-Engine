"Sentimental" is a Porcupine Tree song. It appears as the fourth track on the 2007 album Fear of a Blank Planet.

An early version of the song's chorus can be found on the track "Normal" from the Nil Recurring EP, which contains the B-Sides of Fear of a Blank Planet. There is a musical reference to the Porcupine Tree song "Trains" as well.

The song was NPR's "Song Of The Day" on June 4, 2007.[1]
