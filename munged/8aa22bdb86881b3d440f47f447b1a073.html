Petite Nevis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Petite Nevis is a tiny island in the Grenadines, off the coast of Bequia. The island is uninhabited, but has been used by whalers to flense their catches as late as the 1960s.

A small isolationist community called Moonhole on nearby Bequia scavenges whale bones from the island for building materials.
