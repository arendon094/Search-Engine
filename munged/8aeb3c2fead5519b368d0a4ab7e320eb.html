"Shine" is the name of a 1991 single by British pop group Five Star, becoming their third and final single for their new record label, Epic.

The single was their third with Epic not to make the UK Top 40 (reaching #53). With this failure, their very last appearance on the UK Top 75 to date, Epic parted ways with Five Star, who would not release another single for the next four years.

UK Catalogue Number: Epic EPC657480
