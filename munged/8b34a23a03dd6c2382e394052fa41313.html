Mirzapur (Bengali: মির্জাপুর) is an Upazila of Tangail District in the Division of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

[edit] Geography

Mirzapur is located at 24.1083° N 90.0917° E . It has 61479 units of house hold and total area 373.89 km².

[edit] Demographics

As of 1991 Bangladesh census[1], Mirzapur has a population of 337496. Males constitute are 50.31% of the population, and females 49.69%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 178312. Mirzapur has an average literacy rate of 32.9% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. [2]

[edit] Administrative

Mirzapur has 13 Unions/Wards, 207 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 219 villages.
