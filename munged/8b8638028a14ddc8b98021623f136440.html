Changzhou Benniu Airport

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Changzhou Benniu Airport (IATA: CZX, ICAO: ZSCG) is an airport in Changzhou, Jiangsu, China.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Air China (Beijing)
  • China Eastern Airlines (Beijing)
  • China Southern Airlines (Guangzhou)
  • Shenzhen Airlines (Changsha, Chongqing, Fuzhou, Guangzhou, Haikou, Harbin, Shenyang, Shenzhen, Xiamen)
  • Xiamen Airlines (Xiamen)
