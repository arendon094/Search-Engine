Coordinates: 21°22′N 74°15′E﻿ / ﻿21.37, 74.25 Nandurbar is a city and a municipal council in Nandurbar district in the Indian state of Maharashtra.

[edit] Geography

Nandurbar is located at 21.37° N 74.25° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 210 metres (688 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census[2], Nandurbar had a population of 94,365. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Nandurbar has an average literacy rate of 72%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 78%, and female literacy is 65%. In Nandurbar, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
