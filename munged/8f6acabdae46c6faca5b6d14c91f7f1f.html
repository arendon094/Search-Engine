Aldama

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the surname 'Aldama'. For other uses, which consist of placenames, see Aldama (disambiguation).

Aldama is a surname shared by several notable people:

  • Andrés Aldama (b. 1956), Cuban boxer
  • Frederick Luis Aldama, U.S. academic
  • Juan Aldama (1774–1811), insurgent leader in the Mexican War of Independence
  • Ignacio Aldama (1769–1811), insurgent leader in the Mexican War of Independence
  • Yamilé Aldama (b. 1972), Sudanese triple-jumper
