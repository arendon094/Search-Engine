Roman Catholic Diocese of Ciudad Guzmán

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Ciudad Guzmán (erected 25 March 1972) is a suffragan diocese of the Archdiocese of Guadalajara.

[edit] Ordinaries

  • Leonardo Viera Contreras (1972 -1977)
  • Sefafín Vásquez Elizalde (1977 -1999) - Bishop Emeritus
  • Braulio Rafael León Villegas (1999 - )
