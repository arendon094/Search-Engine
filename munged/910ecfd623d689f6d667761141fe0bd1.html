K (New York City Subway service)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

K or KK was the label for the following New York City Subway services:

  • K (Broadway Brooklyn Local), earlier KK, discontinued in 1976
  • K (Eighth Avenue Local), replaced the AA in 1986 and merged into the C in 1988
