An Evening with Ronnie Drew

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ronnie Drew recorded this album with Mike Hanrahan while on tour in Europe in the autumn of 2004.


Tracklisting:

  1. Finnegan's Wake;
  2. McAlpine's Fusiliers;
  3. Waltzing Matilda;
  4. Two Island Swans;
  5. Firefighter;
  6. The Auld Triangle;
  7. Nora;
  8. The Sick Note;
  9. Someone Like You;
  10. La Quinta Brigada;
  11. We Had It All;
  12. Dicey Reilly;
  13. Dirty Old Town;
  14. Parting Glass
