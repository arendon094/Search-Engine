"I Wanna Live" is a song recorded by the Christian pop punk and power pop band, Stellar Kart. It is the latest single by the group and is the only single they have released within the year 2007. This is the eleventh song on their second album, We Can't Stand Sitting Down.

The song talks about being lazy and not been bothered with life in the first verse, but the chorus and the rest of the song is where the person decides to live to the full.[citation needed]

"I Wanna Live" is also the title of an unrelated song on the album Halfway To Sanity by the Ramones.
