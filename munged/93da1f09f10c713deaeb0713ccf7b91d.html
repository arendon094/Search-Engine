Chantay Savage singles chronology
If You Believe Betcha'll Never Find

"If You Believe" was the first single from Chantay Savage's debut album ...Here We Go. The single performed poorly on the Billboard charts, peaking at No.75. The song appeared in the 1995 film Party Girl.
