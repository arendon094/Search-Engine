Jolo, Sulu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the municipality. For the island, see Jolo Island.

Jolo is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Sulu, Philippines. It is the capital municipality of Sulu. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 87,998 people in 12,814 households.

Jolo was the center of the government of the Sultanate of Sulu. While Manila was just a small settlement, Jolo was already a developing city.

It is located on Jolo Island.

The incumbent Mayor is Mayor Hussein Amin.

[edit] Barangays

Jolo is politically subdivided into 8 barangays.

  • Alat
  • Asturias
  • Bus-Bus
  • Chinese Pier
  • San Raymundo
  • Takut-Takut
  • Tulay
  • Walled City
