Naganohara (長野原町, Naganohara-machi?) is a town located in Agatsuma District, Gunma, Japan.

As of September 1, 2007, the town has an estimated population of 6,361 and a density of 47.50 persons per km². The total area is 133.93 km².

[edit] Geography

Located in the northwestern portion of Gunma Prefecture, almost all of the town has an elevation higher than 500m. The town has Mount Shirane to the north and Mount Asama to the south.

  • Rivers: Agatsuma River, Shirasuna River, Kuma River
