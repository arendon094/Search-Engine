Leonard Mitzel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Leonard Mitzell is a politician and farmer from Alberta, Canada.

Leonard was elected to his first term in the Legislative Assembly of Alberta as the Progressive Conservative member for Cypress-Medicine Hat. He is a current back bencher in the government caucus.

Prior to provincial political career, he took over his family farm in 1977 after his father died.
