Flycatcher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are various families of bird termed flycatchers:

  • The Old World flycatchers, Muscicapidae
  • The Tyrant-flycatchers, Tyrannidae
  • The Monarch flycatchers, Dicruridae
  • The Silky-flycatchers, Ptilogonatidae

Flycatcher could also refer to:

  • The story Flycatcher, a version of the Frog Prince
