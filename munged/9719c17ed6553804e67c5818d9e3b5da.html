The Get Loose Tour is Nelly Furtado's 2007 world concert tour in support of her 2006 album, Loose. The April 4 show in Toronto, Ontario was called Furtado's "Homecoming Concert" and was filmed for a live concert CD/DVD combination released on December 4, 2007.

[edit] Introduction

The concert involved four back-up dancers and four costume changes, including one after Do It, one after All Good Things (Come to an End), and one for the encore. During the European and Canadian legs of the tour, the concert opened with "Afraid" playing while the Nelly Furtado title on the helicopter from the Say It Right video appeared on a large screen. The band then proceeded to perform Say It Right as the large screen lifted up to reveal Furtado singing at a microphone. During the U.S. leg, a large disco ball replaced the screen for the opening, raising to reveal Furtado singing. Also, the European and Canadian legs of the tour featured two large palm trees as part of the stage set up, while these palm trees did not appear in the U.S. tour.
