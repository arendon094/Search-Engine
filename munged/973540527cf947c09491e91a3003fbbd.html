John Molo is a rock and jazz drummer. He has played with many different bands and musicians, including Bruce Hornsby and the Range, The Other Ones, Phil Lesh and Friends, John Fogerty, Keller Williams, Mike Watt, Paul Kelly, David Nelson, Jemimah Puddleduck, and Modereko.

Molo was a member of Bruce Hornsby and the Range when the band won the Grammy Award for Best New Artist of 1986. After the Range disbanded in 1990, Molo continued to collaborate and tour with Hornsby until 2000.
