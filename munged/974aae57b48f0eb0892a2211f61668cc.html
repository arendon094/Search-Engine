"Je lui dirai" (meaning "I Will Tell Him") is the third promotional single from Céline Dion's Miracle album. It was released on October 11, 2004 in France and Belgium.

Dion in the "Je lui dirai" music video (2004).

"Je lui dirai" was written by Jean-Jacques Goldman and produced by Erick Benzi. The song was originally featured on the 2003 album 1 fille & 4 types.

On October 1, 2003 Céline Dion taped the 1 fille & 4 types TV special at the Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, where she performed this song among others.

The music video was directed by Scott Floyd Lochmus and released on October 25, 2004. It shows the making of the Miracle: A Celebration of New Life book.

"Je lui dirai" peaked at #22 on the Belgian Wallonie Airplay chart.
