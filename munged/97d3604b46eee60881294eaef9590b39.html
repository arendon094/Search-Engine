Astrid Løken

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Astrid Løken (14. april 1911 - 19. january 2008) was a Norwegian entomologist and a resistance fighter in the World War II. She was the first woman to become a member of the Norwegian Entomological Society, and specialized in the bumblebee genus Bombus.
