Evening Snow

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Evening Snow was an American Quarter Horse born in 1993. He was sired by Chicks Beduino and was out of the Champion mare Florentine. In 1996 he became the first horse of any breed to run a quarter mile from a standing start and a closed gate in under 21 seconds, finishing with 20.94 and setting a new world record. This earned a speed index of 128.
