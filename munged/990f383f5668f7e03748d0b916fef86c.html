The Canton of Albertville-Sud is a French administrative subdivision, situated in the Savoie département and the Rhône-Alpes région. In 1999, the population was 9435.

The Canton of Albertville-Sud groups together the following communes:

  • Albertville-Sud
  • Cevins
  • Esserts-Blay
  • Gilly-sur-Isère
  • Grignon
  • La Bâthie
  • Monthion
  • Rognaix
  • Saint-Paul-sur-Isère
  • Tours-en-Savoie
