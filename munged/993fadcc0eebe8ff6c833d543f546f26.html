York Regional Road 76

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

York Regional Road 76 or Main Street in Schomberg, Ontario, or King, Ontario. The street is served as to smoothen traffic turning from Highway 27 (Ontario) to Highway 9 (Ontario). Its direction heads east-west between Schomberg Library and Highway 27 (Ontario), then turn and head north-south between Schomberg Library and Highway 9 (Ontario). North of Highway 9 (Ontario) it is referred to as Sideroad 20 in Simcoe County, Ontario.

[edit] York Regional Road 76 Summary

  • Road Name:Main Street or York Regional Road 76
  • City it passes by: King Township, Ontario
  • Distance: Approximate 1.6 km (measured 6.25 cm on MapArt's Map)
  • Major Intersection:Highway 27 (Ontario), Church Street (Schomberg), Highway 9 (Ontario)
  • Attractions: Schomberg Library, Schomberg Community Arena
  • Zonings: Light Residential

[edit] Original purpose

York Regional Road 76 served to provide people convenience in the community of Schomberg, Ontario. It is also serve to smoothen turning traffic between Highway 27 (Ontario), and Highway 9 (Ontario).

[edit] Roads intersecting York Regional 76

  • York Regional Road 27, where the street terminates
  • Moore Park Drive / Cooper Drive
  • Church Street
  • Dr. Kay Drive
  • Western Avenue
  • Highway 9 (Ontario), where the street terminates

[edit] Major Attractions

  • Schomberg Library
  • Schomberg Community Arena

[edit] Zonings

Along the road are all light residential zones. There are about 50 dwellings.
