St. Elizabeth Academy (St. Louis, Missouri)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

St. Elizabeth Academy High School is a private, Roman Catholic high school in St. Louis, Missouri. It is located in the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Saint Louis.

[edit] Background

St. Elizabeth Academy was established in 1882 by the Sisters of the most Precious Blood. [1]
