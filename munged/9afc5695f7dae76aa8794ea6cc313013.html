India in motion

India In Motion is a special 30-minute introductory film. In the movie actor Anupam Kher takes the audiences through India's evolution from an ancient civilization to a modern nation.

The audience will pass through today's India in - or on - a variety of typical vehicles and see the historical events at sites like Mohenjo Daro, Indraprastha and the Taj Mahal, experiencing the bumpy elephant rides with the wind blowing through their hair, or the swaying boat with salty spray on their faces.

The movie would be screened in the Interactive Theatre, located near the Taj Mahal in Agra, which is the first ever interactive cinema theatre in the world, each viewer holds a wireless remote unit with push buttons and a small LCD screen, enabling them to participate in a trivia game about the theme of the film.
