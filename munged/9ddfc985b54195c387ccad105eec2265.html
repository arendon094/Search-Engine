William A. Coulter

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Adirondack architect, see William L. Coulter (1865 – 1907). For the Celtic guitarist, see William Coulter (1959 – ).

William A. Coulter, born William Alexander Coulter, (March 7, 1849 - March 13, 1936) was an American painter of marine subjects.
