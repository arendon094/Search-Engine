Widerøe destinations

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Norwegian airline Widerøe has flights to the following destinations. The Dash 8 aircraft operated by Widerøe are well-suited for many of the smaller regional airports in Norway which have short runways and limited airport facilities.
