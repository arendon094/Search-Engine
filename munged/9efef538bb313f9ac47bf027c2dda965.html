Opció k-95

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Opció k-95 is a Catalan streetpunk band formed in Barcelona in September 1995.

The original line up was Carles (vocals), Daniel (bass) and Raül (drums). Marc (guitar) and Lluís (guitar) joined later. Their lyrics include praise for the liberation of the so-called Catalan Countries (Països Catalans). They consider themselves redskins, anti-fascists and Catalan patriots.
