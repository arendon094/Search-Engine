Made in New York

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Made in New York is an incentive program by which any film or television show with 75% or greater of their work taking place in New York City gains several bonuses from the city government. Such bonuses include tax credits, cheaper advertising within the greater New York area, discount cards, and the right to use the trademarked "Made in New York" logo.
