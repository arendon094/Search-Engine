"Don't Go Gently" is a single by Four Day Hombre, released through Alamo Music in 2006. Available on 7" vinyl (catalogue number ALM V003) and through iTunes.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Don't Go Gently"
  2. "Give Me a Rocket Pack and Show Me the Moon"

The iTunes download does not include "Give Me a Rocket Pack and Show Me the Moon", but instead is accompanied by two other tracks ("It's Okay to Lie" and "Single Room") recorded live at the City Varieties, Leeds, on September 22, 2005.

The title of this song is presumably inspired by the Dylan Thomas poem Do not go gentle into that good night, with which it has some commonality of theme.
