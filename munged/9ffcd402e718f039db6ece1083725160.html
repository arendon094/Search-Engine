Occupation actress

"Go Eun-ah (고은아)", with the real name of Bang Hyo Jin (방효진) is a known South Korean actress who is most known as Keum Shil in Golden Apple (TV series). She debutted in Orione Chocopie CF and she graduated Dressmaking High School. She likes to jazz dance, model and act. Unlike other actors and actresses, she was born in a poor village.
