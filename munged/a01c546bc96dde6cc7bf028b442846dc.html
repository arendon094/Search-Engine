Alain Junior Ollé Ollé

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alain Junior Ollé Ollé (born 11 April 1987 in Douala) is Cameroonian professional footballer currently playing for German club SC Freiburg, for whom he signed from Club Nacional de Football. He plays in midfield.

  • 2005-2006 Plaza Colonia
  • 2006-2007 Águilas CF
  • 2007-2008 Club Nacional de Football
  • 2008- SC Freiburg
