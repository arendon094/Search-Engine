Alejandro Illescas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro Illescas (died 17 January 2008) was a Mexican voice actor who was heard in the Latin American dubs of many movies, TV series, and cartoons, often in small roles as policemen, reporters, or announcers.
