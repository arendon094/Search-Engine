Bill Irwin (baseball)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Irwin was a baseball player for the Cincinnati Red Stockings from 1886 to 1886. He batted right and threw right handed. He was 6'0 and weighed 195 pounds. He was born in Neville, Ohio on September 16, 1859 and died on August 7, 1933 in Fort Thomas, Kentucky.
