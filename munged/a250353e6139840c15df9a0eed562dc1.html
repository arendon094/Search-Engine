Aleksandar Radosavljević (goalkeeper)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aleksandar Radosavljević (Александар Радосављевић) (born December 22, 1982) is a Serbian football goalkeeper. As of July 2007, he plays for FK Partizan. He has previously played for FK Čukarički Stankom.
