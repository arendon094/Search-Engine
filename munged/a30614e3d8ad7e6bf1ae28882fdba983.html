Kangchenjunga (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kangchenjunga may refer to:

  • Kangchenjunga, a mountain on the Nepalese/Indian border.
  • Kanchenjungha, a film by Satyajit Ray.
  • Kanchenjunga (programming tool), a visual rapid application development tool for producing Java client applications for use with Postgres 95.
  • Kanchenjunga, the name given by the Swallows and Amazons to a mountain they climb in Arthur Ransome's book Swallowdale.
