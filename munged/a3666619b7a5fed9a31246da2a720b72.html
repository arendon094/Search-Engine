Pintuyan, Southern Leyte

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pintuyan is a 5th class municipality in the province of Southern Leyte, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 9,567 people in 1,758 households.

[edit] Barangays

Pintuyan is politically subdivided into 23 barangays.
