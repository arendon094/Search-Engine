R. E. Edlefsen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

R. E. Edlefsen
Mayor of Boise, Idaho
In office
May 1951 – May 1959
Preceded by Potter P. Howard
Succeeded by Robert L. Day

Russell E. Edlefsen served four terms as mayor of Boise, Idaho, from 1951 to 1959.
