Gmina Dziemiany is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kościerzyna County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. Its seat is the village of Dziemiany, which lies approximately 19 kilometres (12 mi) south-west of Kościerzyna and 70 km (43 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.

The gmina covers an area of 124.97 square kilometres (48.3 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 4,065.

[edit] Neighbouring gminas

Gmina Dziemiany is bordered by the gminas of Brusy, Karsin, Kościerzyna, Lipusz and Studzienice.
