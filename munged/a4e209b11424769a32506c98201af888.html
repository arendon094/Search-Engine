Menlo Park Terrace, New Jersey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Menlo Park Terrace. It's an unincorporated area within Woodbridge Township in Middlesex County, New Jersey. It is located off U.S, Route 1 in between Menlo Park Mall and Woodbridge Center. It is a residential suburban neighborhood with an ethnically diverse population, a fire station, and Menlo Park Terrace School #19, which services grades K-5. Menlo Park Terrace is located off exit 130 off the Garden State Parkway, and can be seen along the parkway heading southbound.

Residents of Menlo Park Terrace use Metuchen, NJ 08840 as a mailing address.
