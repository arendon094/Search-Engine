Cris Velasco is a composer whose work has been featured on video games and major motion pictures. He has been featured in the God of War video game series, with four tracks on God of War soundtrack and four on God of War II soundtrack. He also composed music for the re-imagined Battlestar Galactica series, Terminator 3: Redemption, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Anderson's Cross, Haze, Splinter Cell: Double Agent (main theme only) and is credited with doing additional music for Van Helsing.
