Yeritsmankants Monastery

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yeritsmankants Monastery, also known as the Monastery of Three Youths, is an Armenian monastery that lies in the northern province of Karabakh, just north of Martakert. Because of the close proximity to the militarized border with Azerbaijan, no visitors are allowed to travel to the monastery. The monastery was built in 1691.
