Mono (from the Greek μόνος (alone) or μονός (single) ) may refer to:

[edit] Biology

  • Infectious mononucleosis, also referred to as "glandular fever", the "kissing disease"
  • Monodactylus sebae, an animal species

[edit] Music

  • Monaural sound production, sound produce from a single channel, in contrast to stereo sound
  • Monophonic, a term relating to monaural sound
  • Mono jack, a jack plug used for transmitting monaural sound
  • Mono (Japanese band), a Japanese post-rock band
  • "Mono", a song by Fightstar from their 2006 album Grand Unification
  • Mono (UK band), a British trip-hop band
  • "Mono" (Courtney Love song), a 2004 Courtney Love song

[edit] Publications

  • mono (Fanzine), Bradford alternative rock music fanzine

[edit] Places

  • Mono County, California
  • Mono Department, Benin
  • Mono Lake
  • Mono, Ontario, Canada
  • Mono River, Togo
  • Mono Island, Solomon Islands

[edit] People

  • Mono language
  • Mono tribe, a tribe of Native Americans in California
  • Mono, an important character in the video game Shadow of the Colossus

[edit] Technology

  • Mono (software), an open source implementation of the Microsoft .NET architecture
  • Monochrome BBS, a bulletin board system originally based at City University, London
  • Everson Mono, also known as Everson Mono Unicode, is an evenly spaced Unicode font (typeface)
  • m0n0wall is an embedded firewall distribution of FreeBSD
  • Mono (game), a shoot 'em up video game reminiscent of both Asteroids and Robotron: 2084
