Choralerna

Background information
Genre(s) Christian

Choralerna, was a gospel-choir from Göteborg, Sweden active from 1968-1981. They were founded and conducted by Lars Brandströmsv. Choralerna was one of Sweden's first and most wellknown gospel-choirs.

They increased their international following by performing Living Water at Eurofest '75 as one of the main attractions.
