Ahava

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahava may refer to:

  • the Hebrew word for love (Hebrew: אהבה)
  • Ahava (company), a cosmeceutical company based in Israel that manufactures skin-care products from the Dead Sea. These products are manufactured using minerals from the Dead Sea – known to have excellent cosmetic qualities. Ahava's products include Facial Products, Cleansing Products, Body Care, Hair Care and Spa at Home for all skin types, from normal to dry and oily. Products for men include an After Shave Lotion, Shampoo for all hair types, and Shower Gel. [1]
  • Ahava river (Aramaic: אהוא) - the river (Ezra 8:21) by the banks of which the Jewish exiles assembled under Ezra when about to return to Jerusalem from Babylon. In all probability this was one of the streams of Mesopotamia which flowed into the Euphrates somewhere in the north-west of Babylonia. It has, however, been supposed to be the name of a place (Ezra 8:15) now called Hit or Tedef, on the Euphrates, east of Aleppo This entry incorporates text from the public domain Easton's Bible Dictionary, originally published in 1897.
