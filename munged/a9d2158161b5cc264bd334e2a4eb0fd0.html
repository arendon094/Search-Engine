List of United States Representatives from Alaska

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of Members of the United States House of Representatives from Alaska in chronology order.

Representative Party District Years District Home Note
Ralph Rivers Democrat AL January 3, 1959 – December 30, 1966 Served as Representative-elect in 1957 and 1958 under the Alaska-Tennessee Plan, pending Alaskan statehood.
Howard Pollock Republican AL January 3, 1967 – January 3, 1971
Nicholas Begich Democrat AL January 3, 1971 – December 29, 1972 Disappeared on a campaign flight from Anchorage to Juneau on October 16, 1972. An official death certificate was issued December 29, 1972.
Don Young Republican AL March 6, 1973 – Elected by special election to fill the vacancy caused by the death of Nick Begich. Current United States Representative.
