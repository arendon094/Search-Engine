James Avalon

James Avalon is a pornographic film director. His aliases include Kunga Sludge, Mondo Tundra and Monty Tundra.[1]

[edit] Awards

  • 2001 AVN Award winner – Best Director (Film) - Les Vampyres[2]
  • 2002 AVN Award nominee – Best Director (Film) - Taboo 2001[3]
  • 2003 AVN Award nominee – Best Director (Film) - Les Vampyres 2[4]
  • 2004 AVN Award nominee – Best Director (Non Feature) - Fantasy[5]
  • 2005 AVN Hall of Fame inductee
  • 2006 Ninfa Prize winner - Best Director - La Mansión del Placer[6]
  • 2007 AVN Award nominee – Best Director (Video) - Sex Pix[7]
  • XRCO Hall of Fame inductee[8]
