Tora Vasilescu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tora Vasilescu (22 March 1951, Tulcea) is a Romanian actress, who has recently appeared in the first Romanian soap opera, "Numai iubirea" ("Only The Love").

She was born in Tulcea and went to local School no. 5 (currently the SOROS kindergarten is located there), and then went on to Highschool no. 2. She showed talent for acting since she was little. After completing her studies at the Theater Institute in Bucharest, she married film director Mircea Daneliuc.
