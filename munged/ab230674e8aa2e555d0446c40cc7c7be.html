Rogério Fidélis Régis or simply Rogério (born February 28, 1976 in Campinas), is a right back. He currently plays for Santo André.

[edit] Honours

  • São Paulo State League: 1996, 2001, 2003
  • Brazilian Cup: 1998, 2002
  • Mercosul Cup: 1998
  • Libertadores Cup: 1999
  • Tournament Rio - São Paulo: 2000, 2002
  • Maria Quitéria Cup: 1997
  • Naranja Cup of Valencia (Spain): 1997

[edit] Contract

  • 20th July 2007 to 15th December 2007
