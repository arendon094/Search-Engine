Delahunt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Delahunt is a family name. It can refer to the following people:

  • Bill Delahunt, a member of the United States House of Representatives
  • Jim Delahunt, a sports presenter
  • Meaghan Delahunt, an Australian novelist

In fiction:

  • Richard Delahunt, a character in the video games Hitman: Contracts and Hitman: Blood Money
This page or section lists people with the surname Delahunt. If an internal link for a specific person referred you to this page, you may wish to add the given name(s) to that link.
