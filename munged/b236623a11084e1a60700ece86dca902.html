Dub Farris Athletic Complex

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Dub Farris Athletic Complex is a sporting complex owned by the Northside ISD located in San Antonio, Texas.

The complex is known for its 10,568-seat football & soccer stadium but it is also home to a large natatorium.
