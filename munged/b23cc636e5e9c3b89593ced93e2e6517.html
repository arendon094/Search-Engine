Oud-Bodegraven

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Oud-Bodegraven in the municipality of Bodegraven.

Oud-Bodegraven (52°5′N, 4°44′E) is a village in the Dutch province of South Holland. It is a part of the municipality of Bodegraven, and lies about 7 km north of Gouda.

The statistical area "Oud-Bodegraven", which also can include the surrounding countryside, has a population of around 110.[1]
