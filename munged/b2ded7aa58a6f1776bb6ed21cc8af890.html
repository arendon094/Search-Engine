Honmaru Kaikan-mae Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Honmaru Kaikan-mae Station (本丸会館前駅, Honmaru Kaikan-mae Eki?) is a city tram station on the Takaoka Kidō Line located in Takaoka, Toyama Prefecture, Japan. The station was renamed from Takaoka Shichō-mae on October 1, 1977.

[edit] Surrounding area

  • Honmaru Kaikan (formerly Takaoka City Office)
