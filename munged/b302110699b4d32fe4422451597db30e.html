Chanel Bermudez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chanel Bermudez is a model who worked for Playboy in the late 1990s.

[edit] Appearances in Playboy special editions

  • Playboy's Blondes, Brunettes, Redheads January 1997 - pages 26-27.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 58 November 1997 - David Rams, pages 48-49.
  • Playboy's Girlfriends July 1998 - pages 38-41.
