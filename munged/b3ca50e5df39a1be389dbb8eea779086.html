Johnny Strzykalski (born December 14, 1922 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, died June 19, 2002 in Hendersonville, North Carolina) was an American football running back for the San Francisco 49ers of the NFL. He was drafted out of Marquette University by the Green Bay Packers in the 1946 NFL Draft.
