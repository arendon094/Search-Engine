Biu Tze

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Biu Tze (鏢指 lit. dart fingers) is the third Wing Chun form, which teaches how to regain and create a new centerline once it's lost, hence sometimes called "emergency techniques". The form is characterized by the use of open hand techniques (as opposed to closed fist punches). The biu tze adds full-torso movements to the arm and leg techniques of the Siu Nim Tao and Chum Kiu forms.
