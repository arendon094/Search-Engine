A creature is a living organism. The word is generally used to refer to non-human animals but does include humans, and it is also sometimes used to mean monster. The word is commonly referred to as creech.

Other uses for creature include:

  • Something which has been created in the theological sense
  • Legendary creature
  • Creature of statute, an organization, such as a corporation, created by statute or treated by statute in some respects as if it were a person separate from its members or owners
