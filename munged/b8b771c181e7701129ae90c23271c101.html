Leptopelis occidentalis is a species of frog in the Hyperoliidae family. It is found in Ivory Coast, Ghana, Liberia, and possibly Nigeria. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, rivers, and intermittent freshwater marshes. It is threatened by habitat loss.
