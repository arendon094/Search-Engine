Shongbigno Pakhikul O Kolkata Bishayak (Bengali: সংবিগ্ন পাখিকুল ও কলকাতাবিষয়ক) was the first album of the Bangla band Moheener Ghoraguli. It was released in 1977 as an Extended play 45 rpm disc by Gathani Records, and had four songs.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Hai Bhalobashi" (Bhalobashi jyosna-e kaashbonay ChhuTtay)
  2. "Shongbigno Pakhikul" (Runway-juDay PoDay aachhe shudhu Keu nei shunyota)
  3. "Kolkata Kolkata"(Bheshe aashe kolkata, kuwasha-tulitay anka...)
  4. "Maroon Shondhyaalok" (Din cholay jaay, shondhyabelay...)
