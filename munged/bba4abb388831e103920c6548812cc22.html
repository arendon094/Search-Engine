Reidun Brusletten

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Reidun Brusletten (b. 1936) is a Norwegian politician for the Christian People's Party. She was Minister of the Secretariat for Development Cooperation in 1983, and Minister of International Development 1984-1986.
