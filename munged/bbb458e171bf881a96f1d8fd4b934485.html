Werner Kiem

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's biathlon
Bronze 1988 Calgary 4 x 7.5 km relay

Werner Kiem (born 30 November 1962) is a former Italian biathlete. Kiem won bronze medals in the 4 x 7.5 km relay with teammates Gottlieb Taschler, Johann Passler and Andreas Zingerle at the 1986 Biathlon World Championships in Oslo and the 1988 Olympic Games in Calgary.
