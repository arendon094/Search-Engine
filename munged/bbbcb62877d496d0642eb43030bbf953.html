Coordinates: 18°13′60″N 78°09′00″E﻿ / ﻿18.2333, 78.15 Lingampet is a village and a Mandal in Nizamabad district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India.[1]

[edit] Geography

Lingampet is located at 18.2333° N 78.1500° E.[2] It has an average elevation of 470 meters (1545 feet).
