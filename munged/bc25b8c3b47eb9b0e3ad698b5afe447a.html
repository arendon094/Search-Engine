No GST Party

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The No Goods and Services Tax Party (usually called No GST), formerly known as Abolish Child Support, is a minor Australian political party that contested candidates in the 2004 Federal election. It was largely a response to the Howard Government's implementation of the Goods and Services Tax. It polled fairly low totals.
