Richard Taylor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Richard Taylor is the name of:

  • Richard Taylor (colonel) (1744–1829), Father of Zachary Taylor
  • Richard Taylor (general) (1826–1879), Confederate general in the American Civil War
  • Richard Taylor (philosopher) (1919–2003), American metaphysical philosopher
  • Richard Taylor (editor) (1781–1858), English naturalist and publisher of scientific journals
  • Richard Taylor (soldier), American Civil War soldier and Medal of Honor recipient
  • Richard R. Taylor (1922–1978), American surgeon general
  • Richard E. Taylor (born 1929), Canadian laureate of the 1990 Nobel Prize in Physics
  • Richard Taylor (UK politician) (born 1934), Independent Kidderminster Member of Parliament
  • Richard Taylor (skater) (1981–2004), Welsh skater
  • Richard Taylor (movies), head of Weta Workshop
  • Richard Taylor (musician), former member of Gin Blossoms
  • Richard Taylor (mathematician) (born 1962), involved in completing the proof of Fermat's last theorem
  • Richard S. Taylor, Nazarene theologian.
  • Richard Taylor (Hollyoaks), a character in UK soap opera Hollyoaks
  • Richard Mansfield Taylor, real name of Jeff Richards (baseball player/actor)
  • Richard John Taylor, British film and television editor

Two Australian rules footballers have been named Richard Taylor:

    • Richard Taylor (footballer) is a former Hawthorn and West Coast Eagles player in the AFL.
    • Dick Taylor (Australian rules footballer) was a player with Melbourne and North Melbourne in the VFL.
