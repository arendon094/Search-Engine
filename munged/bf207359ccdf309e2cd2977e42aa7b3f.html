Belonophora is a genus of plant in family Rubiaceae. It contains the following species:

  • Belonophora ongensis, S.E.Dawson & Cheek
  • Belonophora talbotii, (Wernham) Keay
  • Belonophora coffeoides, Hook.f.
  • Belonophora coriacea, Hoyle
  • Belonophora wernhamii, Hutch. & Dalziel

Five further species names are now considered to be a singele subspecies of Belonophora coffeoides, Hook.f., Belonophora coffeoides, Hook.f. subsp. hypoglauca (Welw. ex Hiern) S.E.Dawson & Cheek.
