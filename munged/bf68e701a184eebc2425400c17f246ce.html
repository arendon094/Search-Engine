Chapter of Purity
Compilation album by Muslimgauze
Released 2002 September
Label Tantric Harmonies TANTRA X9

Chapter of Purity is a compilation CD album by Muslimgauze. Artwork By - o.l.e.g. For Tanta Gfx. First edition: limited edition of 900 in a jewel case version with a gatefold insert (September 2002). Second edition: limited edition of 300 in a gatefold paper cover (200 silver colour and 100 gold colour), with a hand numbered business card size insert (August 2003). The tracks on this compilation CD were selected from the very early Muslimgauze vinyl releases once out on his own Limited Editions label. On the first release (09/2002) track 10 "Chatila" is misspelled "Chatilav", but is correct on the other releases. Dedicated to the Land of Palestine.

[edit] Track origins

  • Tracks 1, 9, & 10 taken from Jazirat-Ul-Arab.
  • Tracks 2, 3, & 8 taken from Blinded Horses.
  • Tracks 4, 5, & 7 taken from Flajelata.
  • Track 6 taken from Hajj.
