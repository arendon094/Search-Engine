Humuleşti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Humuleşti is a village in Neamţ County, Moldavia, Romania, 100km from Iaşi.

Ion Ştefănescu, later Ion Creangă (having taken his mother's maiden name), was born in Humuleşti in 1837. Although not prolific, Creangă is an esteemed Romanian author, noted for his folk tales and childhood reflections.
