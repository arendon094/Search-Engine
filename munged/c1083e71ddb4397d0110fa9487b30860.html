Mount Black Prince (Antarctica)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mount Black Prince
Elevation 3,405 metres (11,171 feet)
Location Victoria Land, Antarctica
Range Admiralty Mountains
Coordinates 71°47′S, 168°15′E

Mount Black Prince is a mountain composed of dark colored rock, which tends to create an imposing appearance. Located 6 km (4 mi) west of Mount Ajax in Victoria Land. Named by the New Zealand GSAE, 1957-58, for its appearance and also for the New Zealand Cruiser HMNZS Black Prince.
