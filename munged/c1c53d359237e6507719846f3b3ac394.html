Juma Volter Mwapachu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Juma Volter Mwapachu is the Secretary General of the East African Community. He replaced the Hon. Amanya Mushega of Uganda who completed his five-year term on March 24, 2006.

He was nominated for the post by His Excellency Jakaya Mrisho Kikwete, President of the United Republic Of Tanzania.

He was elected as Secretary General of the East African Community by the Summit of EAC heads of state on April 5, 2006. Prior to this appointment, Mwapachu was Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary and Tanzania's permanent delegate to UNESCO.

Mwapachu is a law graduate of the University of Dar es Salaam.
