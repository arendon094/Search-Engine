Brady Brus

Brady Brus is the president of and chief meteorologist for Oklahoma City television station KSBI.

Brus' company, Family Broadcasting Group, purchased KSBI in 2004 and also owns America One affiliate KXOC-LP. He was formerly a meteorologist at Oklahoma City's CBS affiliate.

He has two older siblings, Brian and Brenda.
