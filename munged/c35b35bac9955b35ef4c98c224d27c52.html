The Jersey national football team is the official football team for the Bailiwick of Jersey. They are not affiliated with FIFA or UEFA, and therefore cannot compete for the FIFA World Cup or UEFA European Football Championship. Since 1905, they have competed with Alderney and Guernsey for the Muratti Vase.
