Environmental enterprise

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Environmental Enterprise refers to environmentally friendly/compatible business. Specifically, an environmental enterprise is a business that produces value in the same manner which an ecosystem does, neither producing waste nor consuming unsustainable resources. The concept is rooted in the well-enumerated theories of Natural capital, the Eco-Economy and Cradle to Cradle.
