The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale, is an upscale enclosed shopping mall located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Originally it was known as Sunrise Key Shopping Center, but in 1980 the center was enclosed, redeveloped and renamed The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale.

Its anchors are Macy's, Dillard's, Neiman Marcus, and Saks Fifth Avenue.

[edit] Anchors

  • Dillard's (192,174 sq. ft.)
  • Macy's (198,433 sq. ft.)
  • Neiman Marcus (80,683 sq. ft.)
  • Saks Fifth Avenue (72,281 sq. ft.)

Saks will close its doors on July 20th 2008.
