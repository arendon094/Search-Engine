The Street Singer was a 1912 short silent film drama. The film starred Earle Foxe and Alice Joyce. It was Foxe's first film, aged seventeen.

The film was made into a musical in 1924 by Frederick Lonsdale, starring Phyllis Dare, and played at the Lyric Theatre.

[edit] Cast

  • Alice Joyce as Pepita
  • Earle Foxe as Karl
  • Mayme Kelso as Mrs. Burleigh
  • Adelaide Lawrence
