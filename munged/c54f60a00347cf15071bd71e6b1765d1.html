Yomogita Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yomogita Station (蓬田駅, Yomogita Eki?) is a railway station on the JR East Tsugaru Line. It is located in Yomogita, Aomori, Japan.

[edit] Station structure

  • 1-way, 1-track, above-ground station.
