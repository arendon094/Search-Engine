Markus Oehlen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Markus Oehlen (born in 1956, in Krefeld) is a German artist. He studied at the Kunstakademie Düsseldorf. His is formative participant of the German art movement Neue Wilde. He took part at the group exhibition Von hier aus – Zwei Monate neue deutsche Kunst in Düsseldorf in 1984. Oehlen was founding member of the band Fehlfarben. Since 2002 he is prof at the Academy of Fine Arts, Munich. He is the brother of Albert Oehlen.
