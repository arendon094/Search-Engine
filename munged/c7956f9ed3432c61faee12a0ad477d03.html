Bart Simpson's Escape from Camp Deadly is a 2D platform game for Game Boy handheld gaming system developed by Imagineering and released by Acclaim in 1991. The game's plot is similar to that of the episode "Kamp Krusty", however the episode didn't air until September 1992.

[edit] Story

Bart and Lisa go to spend the summer at camp. However the camp is the dangerous Camp Deadly; a camp run by a distant relative of Mr. Burns known as Ironfist Burns. His intentions are to make sure that the children stay in line and have as little fun as possible. At the end of the day Bart realizes that it is too dangerous to stay there, and he makes a night-time escape over Mount Deadly and through a dark forest.

[edit] Game areas

  • Flag capture
    • Both the Morning Activity and Afternoon Activity are games of flag capture in a dense forest.
  • Food breaks
    • The lunch and dinner breaks are a run through a cafeteria filled with food-tossing counselors.
  • Mount Deadly
    • Bart's escape which takes place up this mountain in the middle of the night.
  • Escape to the power station
    • The game's final stage takes place in a forest similar to the early stages, except it's the middle of the night.
