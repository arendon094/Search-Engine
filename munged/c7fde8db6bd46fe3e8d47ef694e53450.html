The Freethinker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Freethinker may be:

  • The Freethinker (journal), the oldest surviving secularist publication in the world, first published in 1881
  • The Freethinker (movie), the English title of a movie called Fritänkaren in Swedish
  • The Freethinker (newspaper), a Whig newspaper founded in 1718 by Ambrose Philips and Hugh Boulter
  • The Freethinker (newsletter), a quarterly newsletter published by Freethought Association of West Michigan
  • The Freethinker (Indian Website), Indian skeptics' website debunking paranormal claims. TheFreethinker.tk
