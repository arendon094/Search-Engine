WSTR

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

WSTR is the call sign of two broadcast stations in the United States:

  • WSTR (FM) ("Star 94"), a radio station in Atlanta, Georgia
  • WSTR-TV, My Network TV's affiliate station in Cincinnati, Ohio
