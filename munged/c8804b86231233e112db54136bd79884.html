Bertolt Flick

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bertolt Martin Flick is the current president and CEO of airBaltic airline. He was born in Germany in 1964, and has law degrees from German and U.S. universities. In 1995 Mr. Flick become a member of airBaltic's Supervisory Board and in 1999 he was elected the Chairman of the Board. Since 2002 he has been the president and CEO of the company.
