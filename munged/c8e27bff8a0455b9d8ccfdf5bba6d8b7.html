Nant Conwy Rugby Football Club (Welsh: Clwb Rygbi Nant Conwy) is a rugby union team from the upper reaches of the Conwy Valley. The club's grounds are located between the town of Llanrwst and the village of Trefriw in North Wales. They presently play in the Welsh Rugby Union Division Four North League. Although Nant Conwy have a short history, they have managed to achieve success on the pitch earning several division promotions in recent years. In 2007 the club applied for funding to install floodlights at their ground.
