Arcos de Valdevez (pron. IPA: ['aɾkuʃ dɨ vaɫdɨ'veʃ]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 447.6 km² and a total population of 24,466 inhabitants (2006). The municipality is composed of 51 parishes, and is located in the district of Viana do Castelo.

The present Mayor is Francisco Rodrigues de Araújo, elected by the Social Democratic Party.

The municipal holiday is July 11.

Atlético de Valdevez is Arcos' local football (soccer) club.
