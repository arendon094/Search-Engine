We Leave At Dawn
Studio album by Envy and Other Sins
Released 31 March 2008
Genre Indie Rock
Length 42:00
Label A&M/Polydor
Producer Danton Supple

We Leave At Dawn is the debut album by British band Envy & Other Sins. It was released on March 31, 2008 through Polydor Records.
