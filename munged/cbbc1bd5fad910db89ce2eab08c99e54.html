Juice bar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

lovejuice at Heathrow Terminal 5

A juice bar is an establishment which primarily serves prepared juice beverages. It shares some of the characteristics of a bar or coffeehouse.

Wheatgrass juice is often included on the menu.
