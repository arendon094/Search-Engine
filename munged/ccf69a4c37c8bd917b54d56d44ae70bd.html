Sure of You (1989) is the sixth book in the Tales of the City series by San Francisco novelist Armistead Maupin.

Though the first five books in the series were originally serialized in the San Francisco Chronicle or the San Francisco Examiner, Sure of You and its follow-up, Michael Tolliver Lives, were written solely as novels.
