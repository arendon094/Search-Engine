Bítovany

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Bítovany in the Czech Republic

Bítovany is a village in the Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. It has around 400 inhabitants.

Hamlet Bítovánky is the administrative part of Bítovany.

The village was founded in 1349. There is very old church which dates back to the 14th century. The rivulet Holetínka passes through the town.
