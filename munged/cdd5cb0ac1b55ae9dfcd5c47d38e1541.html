Amandine (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amandine may refer to:

  • Amandine (culinary term), a French culinary term for a garnish of almonds
  • Amandine potato, a type of potato
  • Amandine (band), a music band

Because of the similar spelling, the word may be confused with almandine, a type of garnet.
