Paszport Polityki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Paszport Polityki (Polityka's Passport) is an annuall prestigious Polish award presented by the weekly magazine Polityka since 1993.

It is presented in six categories: literature, film, theatre, classical music, visual arts and scene. In 2002 a new, seventh category was added: creator of culture.
