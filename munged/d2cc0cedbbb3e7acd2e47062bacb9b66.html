Americanos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For a definition of the word "americanos", see the Wiktionary entry americanos.

"Americanos" is the second single from former Frankie Goes to Hollywood singer Holly Johnson, from his 1989 debut album Blast. It peaked at #4 on the UK singles chart, as did his previous single "Love Train". Blast contained two more singles, one of which did not make the UK Top 40.
