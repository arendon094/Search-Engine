Sporocarp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sporocarp can refer to any of several structures whose primary function is the production and release of spores.

  • Sporocarp (fungi) (also known as fruiting body), a multicellular structure on which spore-producing structures are borne.
  • Sporocarp (ferns), specialized spore-producing tissues found on the sporophyte.
