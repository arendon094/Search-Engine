WACM-LP (94.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Auburn, Alabama. The station is owned by the Auburn Chinese Ministry Association. It airs a Religious radio music format.[1] It derives a portion of its programming as part of the Footprint Radio Broadcasting Ministry.[2]

The station was assigned the WACM-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 11, 2004.[3]
