2005 ATP Tour

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of the tournaments played in the 2005 season of Men's tennis (calendar year), including ATP Tour events and ITF events (This does not include the ITF Men's Circuit, only the ATP circuit).

  • Number of Tournaments played on hardcourts: 31 (2 Grand Slams)
  • Number of Tournaments played on clay: 24 (1 Grand Slam)
  • Number of Tournaments played on grass: 6 (1 Grand Slam)
  • Number of Tournaments played on carpet: 6
