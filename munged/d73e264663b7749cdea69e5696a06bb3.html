Water buffalo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Water buffalo can refer to:

  • Domestic buffalo, the abundant Domestic Asian Water buffalo
  • Wild Asian Water Buffalo, the endangered species which is the domestic buffalo's ancestor
  • The fish called the freshwater drum is sometimes known as the river buffalo
  • See Water buffalo incident for a controversy at the University of Pennsylvania
  • The water buffalo, a Vietnamese zodiac sign corresponding to the Chinese ox
