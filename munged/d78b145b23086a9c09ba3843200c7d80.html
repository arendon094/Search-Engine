Ratnagiri Hindu Sabha

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Ratnagiri Hindu Sabha was an organization founded by Indian revolutionary Vinayak Damodar Savarkar in 1924. Based out of Ratnagiri (in modern Maharashtra), the Sabha worked for the preservation of Hindu social and cultural heritage. It later merged into the Hindu Mahasabha.
