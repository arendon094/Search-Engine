Sol Butler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Edward Solomon "Sol" Butler (March 3, 1895 – 1954) was an American track and field athlete who competed in the 1920 Summer Olympics.

In 1920 he finished seventh in the long jump competition.
