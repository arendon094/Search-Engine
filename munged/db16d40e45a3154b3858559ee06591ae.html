Gan Raveh Regional Council

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gan Raveh Regional Council (Hebrew: מועצה אזורית גן רווה‎, Mo'atza Azurit Gan Raveh) is a regional council on the Mediterranean coast in the Center District of Israel. The council is located between Rishon LeZion to the north, Ness Ziona, Rehovot and Brenner Regional Council to the east, Yavne and Hevel Yavne Regional Council to the south. It covers eight settlements; a kibbutz, six moshavim and a youth village. Established in 1952, it had a population of 5,000 in 2006.

[edit] List of settlements

  • Ayanot (youth village)
  • Beit Hanan (moshav)
  • Beit Oved (moshav)
  • Ge'alya (moshav)
  • Gan Sorek (moshav)
  • Kfar HaNagid (moshav)
  • Neta'im (moshav)
  • Palmachim (kibbutz)
