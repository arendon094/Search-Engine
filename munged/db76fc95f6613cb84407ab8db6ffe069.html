The Fuck Buddy is the fourteenth episode of the second season of the HBO television series Sex and the City. It originally aired on September 5, 1999.

“ A fuck buddy is a guy you probably dated once or twice and it didn't really go anywhere, but the sex is so great you sort of... keep him on call. ”

Carrie tries to make a relationship with her "fuck buddy." Miranda dates a mean, condescending lawyer. Charlotte double-books dates with two guys on the same night. Samantha overhears the couple moaning next door, and decides to outdo them.

In Syndicated reruns of the episode, in order to conform with FCC standards, and to make the show "Broadcast Friendly" the name of the episode was changed to The Sex Buddy.
