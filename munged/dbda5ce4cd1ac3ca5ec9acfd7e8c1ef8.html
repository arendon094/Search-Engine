"Say Say Say (Waiting 4 U)" is the debut single by Hi_Tack, released in 2006. It reached a peak position of #4 on the UK singles chart. The song samples the classic 1983 song Say Say Say by Paul McCartney and Micheal Jackson.

  2. "Say Say Say (Waiting 4 U)" (Original)
  3. "Say Say Say (Waiting 4 U)" (Tocadisco's Not Guilty Remix)
  4. "Say Say Say (Waiting 4 U)" (Delano Crocketts Late Nite Dub Mix)
  5. "Beats 4 U"
