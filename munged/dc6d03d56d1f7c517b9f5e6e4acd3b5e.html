Enayetpur

Enayetpur, The latest thana of Sirajgonj. The river Jamuna is passing just beside it. Enayetpur is famous for an Islamic saint named Shah Sufi Khaja Yunus Ali Enayetpuri. This thana has also some villages which are famous for handloom. Here the sharees(female dress), Lungis(male dress) are produced. Among the villages Gopalpur, Rupnai, Khukni, Gopinathpur are main. Most of the people here are related with the loom.

Recently a rich hospital named "KHAJA YUNUS ALI MEDICAL COLLEGE AND HOSPITAL" is providing health services to the people of the whole district as well as other part of the country.
