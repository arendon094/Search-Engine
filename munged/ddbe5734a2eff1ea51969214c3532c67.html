Selnik

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Selnik (Macedonian: Селник) is a village in the Republic of Macedonia. It belongs to the Delčevo municipality and is situated on one of the mountains next to the town of Delčevo. Parts of the village are named after old family clans whoe settled there long time ago, for example Lesovci (named after the family name Lesovski).
