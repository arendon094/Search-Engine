Edmond Aman-Jean

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

E. Aman-Jean: Miss Ella Carmichaël, 1906

Edmond Aman-Jean (1858 - 1936) was a French symbolist painter, who founded the Salon des Tuileries. It is also noted that he was a friend of Georges Seurat.[citation needed]
