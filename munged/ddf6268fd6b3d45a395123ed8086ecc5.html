FA Cup 1876-77

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Football Association Challenge Cup 1876-77 was the sixth staging of the FA Cup, England's oldest football tournament. Thirty-nine teams entered, seven more than the previous season, although seven of the thirty-nine never actually played a match.
