The Diocese of Joaçaba {Latin: Ioassaben(sis)} is a diocese located in the city of Joaçaba in the Ecclesiastical province of Florianópolis in Brazil.

[edit] History

  • June 12, 1975: Established as Diocese of Joaçaba from the Diocese of Caçador, Diocese of Chapecó and Diocese of Lages

[edit] Leadership

  • Bishops of Joaçaba (Roman rite)
    • Bishop Walmir Alberto Valle, I.M.C. (2003.04.09 – present)
    • Bishop Osório Bebber, O.F.M. Cap. (1999.03.17 – 2003.04.09)
    • Bishop Henrique Müller, O.F.M. (1975.06.27 – 1999.03.17)
