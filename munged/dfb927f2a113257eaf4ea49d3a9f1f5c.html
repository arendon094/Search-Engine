Red Shirt, South Dakota

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Red Shirt is a small unincorporated Lakota village southeast of Hermosa in Shannon County, South Dakota, United States. It is on the Pine Ridge Reservation, just outside Badlands National Park.
