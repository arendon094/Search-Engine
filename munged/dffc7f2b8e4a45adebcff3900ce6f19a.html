Mayors of Oxford

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The first known mayor of Oxford in England was probably Laurence Kepeharm (1205–1207?). Since 1962, there has been a lord mayor of Oxford. The current incumbent is John Tanner from the Labour Party.
