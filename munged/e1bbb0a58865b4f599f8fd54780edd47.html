Live at 25 is a live album by Huey Lewis & The News celebrating the 25 year anniversary since the band's formation in 1980. The performance was recorded December 2004 in the band's home turf of Northern California and was released along with a separate Live at 25 DVD in 2005 by Rhino Records.
