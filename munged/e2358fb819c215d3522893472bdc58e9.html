Don Laughlin's Riverside Resort Hotel & Casino is located in Laughlin, Nevada, on the Colorado River. It was the first hotel and casino constructed in the town. The resort features 1,400 rooms, a casino with a race and sports book, 34-lane bowling center, and a 6 screen movie theater.

The resort also has a full-service RV park with 740 spaces, as well as several restaurants. The hotel has its own yacht, The U.S.S. Riverside, which is available for charter.
