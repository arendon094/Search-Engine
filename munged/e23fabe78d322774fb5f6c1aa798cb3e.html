Asturicani

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Asturicani are a tribe mentioned by Ptolemy (v. 9. § 7) as dwelling adjacent to the Pontus Euxinus (Black Sea). William Smith equates them with the Aspurgiani.

  • Smith, Dictionary of Greek and Roman Geography (1857).
