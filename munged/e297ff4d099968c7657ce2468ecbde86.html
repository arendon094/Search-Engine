Funairi-machi is a Hiroden station (tram stop) on Hiroden Eba Line located in Funairi-machi, Naka-ku, Hiroshima.

[edit] Routes

From Funairi-machi Station, there are two of Hiroden Streetcar routes.

  • Hiroshima Station - Eba Route
  • Yokogawa Station - Eba Route
