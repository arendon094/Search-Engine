Margaretha de Mol (born July 8, 1964 in Hilversum), known as Linda de Mol, is a Dutch actress and television presenter in the Netherlands and in Germany. She is the sister of Endemol founder John de Mol. She has her own magazine Linda..

[edit] Television

Linda de Mol presented The DJ Kat Show (1985–1995) and Coca-Cola Eurochart Top 50 on Sky Channel, one of Endemol's first productions. Later she presented the shows or quizzes such as Miljoenenjacht (the original version of Deal or No Deal) and De Slimste, and M/V. She can also be seen in the weekly drama series Gooische Vrouwen on RTL4 (first Tien).
