Fallen nature

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fallen nature is a key concept in some major religions.

In Unification Theology, the four fallen natures are:

  1. Failure to understand or love from God's point of view
  2. Leaving one's proper position (refusing to receive God's love through the Abel-type person)
  3. Reversal of dominion
  4. Multiplication of evil
