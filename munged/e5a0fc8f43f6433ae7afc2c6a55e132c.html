The Roman Catholic Diocese of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim {Latin: Cachoëiren(sis) de Itapemirim} is a diocese located in the city of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim in the Ecclesiastical province of Vitória in Brazil.

[edit] History

  • February 16, 1958: Established as Diocese of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim from the Diocese of Espírito Santo

[edit] Leadership

  • Bishops of Cachoeiro de Itapemirim (Roman rite)
    • Bishop Célio de Oliveira Goulart, O.F.M. (2003.07.09 – present)
    • Bishop Luiz Mancilha Vilela, SS.CC. (later Archbishop) (1985.12.03 – 2002.12.03)
    • Bishop Luís Gonzaga Peluso (1959.07.25 – 1985.12.03)
