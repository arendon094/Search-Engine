Cataraqui River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cataraqui River (pronounced "ka-tah-RAH-kway") forms the lower portion of the Rideau Canal and drains into Lake Ontario at Kingston, Ontario. It is also known as the Great Cataraqui River or the Greater Cataraqui River to distinguish it from the smaller Little Cataraqui Creek, 4.5 km to the west. The Cataraqui River system was extensively modified during the construction of the canal: swamps were turned into lakes and new marshes were formed.

The Cataraqui Region Conservation Authority administers water management concerns within the Cataraqui River watershed.
