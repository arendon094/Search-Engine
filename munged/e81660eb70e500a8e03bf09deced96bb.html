Rep. Bruce Goforth

Daniel Bruce Goforth is a Democratic member of the North Carolina General Assembly representing the state's one hundred fifteenth House district, including constituents in Buncombe county. A contractor from Asheville, North Carolina, Goforth is currently (2003-2004 session) serving in his first term in the state House.
