Sunshine Square

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sunshine Square is a big shopping mall containing a supermarket and a departmental store in Bayan Lepas, Penang, Malaysia. It has a multi-storey car park, a bowling centre and a food court. Sunshine Square's new wing (an extension of the supermarket), was opened to public in 2005. Sunshine Square attracts many people during weekends, school holidays, and public holidays[citation needed].
