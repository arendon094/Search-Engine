Lavrentis Dianellos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lavrentis Dianellos (Greek: Λαυρέντης Διανέλλος, 1911 in Agios Lavrentios in Magnesia – 1978 in Seattle, Washington) was a Greek actor. From 1948 to 1975 he appeared in 200 films leading one reviewer to call him "ubiquitous."
