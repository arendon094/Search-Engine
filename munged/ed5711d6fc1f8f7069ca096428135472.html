Kortrijk (Netherlands)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Kortrijk in the municipality of Breukelen.

Kortrijk (52°9′N, 4°59′E) is a hamlet in the Dutch province of Utrecht (province). It is a part of the municipality of Breukelen, and lies about 10 km northwest of Utrecht.

The statistical area "Kortrijk", which also can include the surrounding countryside, has a population of around 270.[1]
