List of King George V Playing Fields (Kent)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Westerham

There are 471 King George V Playing Fields[1] in the United Kingdom, all part of an enormous memorial to King George V, each of which is an individual registered charity[2]. This is the page for Kent.
