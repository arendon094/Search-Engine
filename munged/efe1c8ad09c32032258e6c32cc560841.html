CyberZone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

CyberZone is a popular Video Dance production located in Southern Ontario. It is a video dance company hosted by CYBERFORCE PRODUCTIONS INC, usually taking place for three to four hours at a time. Typical admission fees are $10 CAN. It often hosts special events, sometimes giving out prizes. Food comes at an extra cost. Typical visitors include people seeking someone of the opposite gender. CyberZone dances are held for general community events, high school dances, fundraisers etc.

The original CyberZone dances took place at Eastview Secondary School in Barrie, Ontario. They now regularly take place at The Lion's Gate Banquet Hall but occasionally change location for special and sporting events. The dances also used to be held at Georgian College. These dances are monitored by adult figures, sometimes local teachers. Sometimes it is also hosted at Sir William Mulock Secondary School in Newmarket, Ontario, as well as in Brantford, Burlington, Belleville, Peterborough, Napanee, Kingston, Alliston and Bowmanville. Line-ups are generally quite long to get into the events, and many participants enjoy the events.

CyberZone dance participants are typically children and teenagers aged 8-14.
