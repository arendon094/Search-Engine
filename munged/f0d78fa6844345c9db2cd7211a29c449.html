Miss Venezuela 2008

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Miss Venezuela 2008
Date: September 10, 2008
Venue: Poliedro de Caracas, Caracas, Venezuela
Broadcaster: Venevision

Miss Venezuela 2008, the 56th Miss Venezuela beauty pageant, will be held on September 10, 2008, at the Poliedro de Caracas in Caracas, Venezuela.[1] Dayana Mendoza, from the state of Amazonas, is the current titleholder.

Corinne Schumacher, a venezuelan niece of Michael Schumacher (former Formula One driver) is one of the official delegates in the pageant.[2][3]

  • Sucre - Erika González
  • Táchira - Jennipher Bortolas
  • Vargas - Leonela Mayora [6]
  • Zulia - Gabriela Fernández [7]
