Ariy Pazovsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ariy Moiseyevich Pazovsky (2 February 1887 - 6 January 1953) was a Russian conductor. As a conductor of the Bolshoi Opera, he is credited with having returned parts of Modest Mussorgsky's opera Boris Godunov which had been censored in Tsarist Russia.
