Sabine Bramhoff

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sabine Bramhoff (born 1 November 1964) is a retired German high jumper.

She finished seventh at the 1989 European Indoor Championships.[1] She represented the sports club LC Paderborn, and won the silver medal at the West German champion in 1989.[2]

Her personal best jump was 1.94 metres, achieved in August 1990 in Düsseldorf.[3]
