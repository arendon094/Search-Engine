Monkey Sanctuary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Monkey Sanctuary was founded in 1964 by Len Williams as a cooperative that would care for rescued woolly monkeys. Based in Looe, Cornwall, the sanctuary has several woolly monkeys that are descended from the original rescued pets, as well as some capuchin monkeys that have been recently rescued from bad conditions.
