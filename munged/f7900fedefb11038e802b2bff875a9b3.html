Progressive Workers' and Farmers' Union

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Progressive Workers' and Farmers' Union (Progressieve Arbeiders- en Landbouwersunie) is a political party in Suriname. At the last legislative elections, held on 25 May 2005, the party won 1.0% of the popular vote and no seats in the National Assembly.
