Consort music

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Consort music is an English Renaissance genre of songs for voice accompanied by a group of instruments as a whole or as broken.

Consort is the English name for a group of instruments, either all of one type (Full consort), or of different types (broken consort)
