Andrew Howarth

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Howarth (born May 18, 1978 in Solihull, England) is an ice-hockey Forward who currently plays for his hometown team, the Solihull Barons. Known as a very physical player, Howarth is often noted for his effort, and checking ability. He has been used as a power forward at every team he's played for. Andrew Howarth is not recognised for his goalscoring ability, but is nonetheless valuable to the barons in using his power to create chances for team mates Erik Bakalar and Neil Adams.
