Siege of Koriyama

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Siege of Koriyama (吉田郡山城の戦い, Siege of Koriyama?) took place from 1540 until 1541 in Yoshida, Aki Province, Japan during the Sengoku period. Amako Haruhisa, with 3,000 men, attacked Koriyama Castle, which belonged to Mōri Motonari and was defended by 8,000 men. When Mōri sent an army to relieve the seige, Amako was forced to leave.
