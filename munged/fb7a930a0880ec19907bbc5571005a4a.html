Josef Horešovský

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Josef Horešovský (born July 18, 1946 in Žilina u Kladna, Czechoslovakia) is a retired professional ice hockey player who played in the Czechoslovak Extraliga. He played for HC Jihlava and HC Sparta Praha. He won a bronze medal at the 1972 Winter Olympics.
