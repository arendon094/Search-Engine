Gloria Muzio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gloria Muzio

Occupation Theatre and television director
Years active 1998-present

Gloria Muzio is an American theatre and television director. She has worked on a number of TV shows including Criminal Minds, Brother and Sisters, The Black Donnellys, The Closer, ER, Third Watch, Law & Order, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, The Wire and Oz.
