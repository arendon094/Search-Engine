Tolve is a town and comune in the province of Potenza, in the Southern Italian region of Basilicata. It is bounded by the comuni of Albano di Lucania, Cancellara, Irsina (MT), Oppido Lucano, San Chirico Nuovo, Tricarico (MT), Vaglio Basilicata.
