The Barrie Lakeshores are Senior "A" box lacrosse team from Barrie, Ontario. The Lakeshores play in the Major Series Lacrosse Senior "A" Lacrosse League.

[edit] History

The Lakeshores began play in the Major Series Lacrosse in 2004, using the nickname from a Barrie team that played in the 1940's.[1]
