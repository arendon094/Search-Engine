St. Lawrence with St. Paul's, Kirkdale is a Church of England church situated in Kirkdale, North Liverpool. Its parish boundary runs from the edge of Bootle to the north down until the edge of the Vauxhall area and to the edge of Walton in the east.

St. Lawrence church joined with St. Paul's in 2002 when St. Paul with St. Mary's, Bootle, was split in two and brought into the Liverpool North Deanery.

It is part of the Liverpool North Deanery, Liverpool Diocese and York Archdiocese.

Liverpool Youth For Christ is currently based in The Shepherd Centre, a specially designed community centre attached to the St. Paul's building.
