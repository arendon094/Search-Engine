Offsiders

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Offsiders is an Australian television sports program, airing on ABC on Sundays at 10.30am.

The show is hosted by Barrie Cassidy, and regular panellists include Gerard Whateley, Jacquelin Magnay, John Harms, Roy Masters, Gideon Haigh and Caroline Wilson.
