Oberhoffen-lès-Wissembourg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Oberhoffen-lès-Wissembourg is a commune in the Bas-Rhin département, close to Wissembourg. Population (1999): 279.

It is the longest-named commune in the Alsace region.
